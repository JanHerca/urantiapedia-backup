by _Geoff Theiss_

Dear friends of _The Urantia Book_ Fellowship, During the past twelve months, our Pipeline of Light volunteers have placed five hundred and fifty one (551) copies of _The Urantia Book_ into twenty-four countries. Your donations to the Pipeline helped us deliver books in response to new requests from readers and study groups in Colombia, Mexico, Zimbabwe, Brazil, Canada, the United States, the Philippines, Venezuela, Uganda, Nigeria, Costa Rica, Chile, Spain, Pakistan, Georgia, and Israel. 

Current efforts bring our Pipeline all-time totals to thirteen thousand, nine hundred and eighty-three (13,983) copies of _The Urantia Book_ into eighty-seven countries and two hundred ancl twenty-one correctional facilities around the world. Many thanks to the wonderful volunteers who keep this flow Of books moving into the world! 

Some additional highlights from this year were: 

- Added the country Georgia, extending the total number Of beneficiary countries to eighty-seven
- The launch Of a dedicated Spanish language unit for the program
- Partnering with a cadre of hub and spoke regional leaders around the world to synchronize Pipeline placements and community development efforts 
- Placement of The Urantia Book into every library in the state of Georgia department of corrections in English and Spanish
- One placement in a chaplain library in Florida inspired the chaplain to sponsor a 16 week course on The Urantia Book in that correctional facility
- Continued saving on shipping costs

We deeply appreciate the contributions to our volunteers and our donors. If you would like to contribute to this work, donations can be made online at:

https://urantiabook.org/donate
