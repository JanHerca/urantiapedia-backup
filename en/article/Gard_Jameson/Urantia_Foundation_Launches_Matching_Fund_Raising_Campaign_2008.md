_By Gard Jameson, Treasurer, Urantia Foundation_

Four anonymous donors have generously pledged to match, dollar-for-dollar, contributions up to $100,000. The world needs _The Urantia Book_, and Urantia Foundation needs your support more than ever.

In the next 12 months, Urantia Foundation will print three new translations: the Estonian, Hungarian, and Swedish. Funds are needed to reprint the Portuguese and Spanish translations. Better means of book distribution throughout the world are essential to spreading the teachings which in turn can powerfully contribute to changing the perspectives of individuals and their relationship to God, and to invigorating the social network of an increasing global society.

The matching fundraising campaign ends on December 31, 2008. Please contact 773-525-3319

or

http://www.urantia.org/contributions.html
