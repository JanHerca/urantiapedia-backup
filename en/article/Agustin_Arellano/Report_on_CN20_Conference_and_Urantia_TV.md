by _Agustín Arellano_

All the conference was live streamed at Urantia TV, Facebook pages, as well as YouTube channel.

Urantia TV is getting all the main TV hours filled with content, we have eight people doing 15 different shows.

<figure id="Figure_2" class="image urantiapedia image-style-align-right">
<img src="/image/article/The_Mighty_Messenger/2020_Fall/041.jpg">
</figure>

Agustin Arellano is producing four TV shows:

1. On Monday nights at 7pm central, he has Encuentro, a show with a therapist in the community.
2. On Wednesday at 7pm central is The Great Library, a talk with authors of secondary works who have been giving their works for Urantia TV so people will be able to download from our page very soon.
3. On Friday night at 7 central we have Celestial Artisans, a show dedicated to those creatives in our midst readers who happen to be artists.
4. Finally, on Saturday he has a board game show, 24 games used from UB glossary and marathon, like a knowledge game.

We started the conference on time on Friday August 21st with 49 people as the highest audience in the zoom room. We had 212 people registered and 26 people who paid for their registration. Agustin mailed 10 packages to the US with the conference kits and will send 4 more after the conference.

Agustin also sent 4 packages with 3 conference kits each to different cities in Mexico. We could not mail more since it was not included in the budget.
