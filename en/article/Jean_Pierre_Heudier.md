## Bio

Jean-Pierre Heudier, Ph.D., is a retired university professor now living in San Marcos, Texas. He has been studying _The Urantia Book_ since 1970, and was instrumental in forming the first study groups in Austin and San Marcos. He has been active as a presenter at Texas conferences and at international conferences, especially those taking place in France, as a presenter/interpreter.
