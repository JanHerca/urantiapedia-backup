by _Zshonette Reed_

TheChristExperiment is a _Urantia Book_ based ministry that “dares to depend solely on Jesus and his incomparable teachings” (UB 195:9.4) in spreading the message of the Fatherhood of God and the Brotherhood of Man. We invite all _Urantia Book_ readers to join together in a worldwide unity project to foster the religion OF Jesus and lead us to light and life.

Learn more about TheChristExperiment and this unity project in the video below.

Video: Honoring Jesus As a United Family

In addition to its flagship ministry, [www.TheChristExperiment.Org](https://thechristexperiment.org/), we also conduct several other ministries with the same objective:

The Christ Experiment's School of Evangelists - Developing leaders and teachers of the Fifth Epochal Revelation [School.TheChristExperiment.org](https://school.thechristexperiment.org/)

Faith Sons of God - A library demonstrating how to uplift traditional Christian thought [www.FaithSonsofGod.org](https://faithsonsofgod.org/)

Uniting Urantia - A mobile app helping Urantia Book readers find other readers near them - [www.UnitingUrantia.org](https://unitingurantia.org/)
