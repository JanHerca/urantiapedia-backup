_Editor's Note: Iremli Ivalo-Sjölie and Werner Sutter hosted a booth at the book fair in Rasatt, Germany this past October._

Many German readers assisted them in the booth, presented Das Urantia Buch to the visitors, and engaged in meaningful discussions about the book's teachings.

The audio version of the German translation was also available.

It was an enjoyable experience for all.
