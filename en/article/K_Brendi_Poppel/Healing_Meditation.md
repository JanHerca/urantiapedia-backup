<p style="text-align:center;">
Creative Life Force coursing through me, <br>
With vibrations of healing energy, <br>
Renewing my body, mind and soul, <br>
Making me a whole, healthy being, <br>
Divinely freeing the child in me, <br>
I am wonderfully wild, spontaneously free... <br>
<br>
I let everything go as the Spirit surges, <br>
With words flowing forth as wisdom emerges, <br>
Intuitive insight empowers my hearing, <br>
Time and space disappearing in the everpresent NOW... <br>
<br>
Lord, show me how to transform and transcend, <br>
Mend my broken spirit, restore my troubled mind, <br>
In the healing, holy presence I know I can find my way <br>
Out of the darkness and into the dawn, <br>
Once more in the Light, I am being reborn, <br>
As I breathe in blessings from God within and above, <br>
<br>
I tune into a universe of infinite love... <br>
</p>

K. Brendi Poppel
