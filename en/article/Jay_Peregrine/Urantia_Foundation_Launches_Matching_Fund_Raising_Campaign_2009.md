_By Jay Peregrine, Executive Director, Urantia Foundation, Illinois, USA_

Five readers of _The Urantia Book_ have agreed to contribute up to $105,000 to Urantia Foundation if we can raise an equal amount of money by the end of the year. Will you help us match their pledge?

In 2010, Urantia Foundation will print the Polish, Swedish, Estonian, and Hungarian translations and will re-print the Spanish and Portuguese translations; revisions and improvements to the Foundation's website will continue; the historic office building at 533 Diversey Parkway will require maintenance and repair; special attention will be given to book distribution in Europe; and improvements to the Urantia Book Internet School will be made.

Will you help insure that the Foundation's work in 2010 continues? Your donation will be doubled (up to $105,000 ) by the matching fund donors if it is received by December 31 , 2009. Please consider sending a donation today. Contact us at 773-525-3319 or at http://urantia.org/donate
