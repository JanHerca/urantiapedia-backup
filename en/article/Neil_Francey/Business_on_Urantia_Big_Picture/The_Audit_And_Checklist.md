## YOUR BUSINESS ACTIVITY AUDIT

&nbsp; | &nbsp;
--- | ---
101 | WHAT ARE THE REAL MOTIVES THAT DRIVE YOU? <br>How can you confirm and measure?
202 | WHAT ARE THE REWARDS EXPECTED? <br>Are these personally and spiritually rewarding?
303 | WHO ARE THE IMPORTANT PEOPLE IN YOUR LIFE? <br>Are they supportive, appreciative, inspiring?
404 | DOES YOUR WORK MAKE YOU TRULY HAPPY? <br>Does it complement emotional and spiritual needs?
505 | HOW WILL YOU EVALUATE YOUR CAREER? <br>This will reveal who you really are, and what you value.
606 | DOES THIS NUMBER MEAN ANYTHING TO YOU? <br>Here's a clue: It's a great place to start your career.
707 | WHAT COMES AFTER SEVEN SUPERUNIVERSES? <br>This is the ultimate corporate ladder.
808 | HOW CAN WE EXPAND BEYOND OUR GLOBAL LIMITS? <br>Perspective is essential to humility and vision.
909 | CAN YOUR LOVE BE GREATER TODAY THAN YESTERDAY? <br>How can this question be answered?

## THE CHECKLIST

### BROTHERHOOD OF MAN

Family, friends, community, customers, employees, competitors, strangers, those who serve you, those in authority, those who seem to know a loving God and those who do not, those who owe you money, investors, those of other races and religions.

### FATHERHOOD OF GOD

Personality expression, the only reality, the presence, life itself, divine will, spirit within, ultimate destiny, the source of love, the wise parent, true friend, the one who is worshiped. Father.
