_Editor's Note: Eugene Asiado and his wife, Belen, are in the Philippines for the second time placing Urantia Books in libraries. This second library project is taking place in the greater Manila area. Urantia Foundation donated 160 English hardcovers for this project, and Urantia Association International donated the shipping funds to get the books to the Philippines._
