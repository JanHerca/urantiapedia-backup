by _David Glass_

<p style="text-align: center;">
With joyful hearts we live within your presence,<br>
You who are everywhere around us now.<br>
We bring you gifts of love and thanks, our presents,<br>
In our appreciation for your vow<br>
To show us all of Truth, although the time<br>
Involved may be of vast near-endless scope.<br>
From seeming chaos unto worlds sublime<br>
We'll climb while you expand our every hope.<br>
And yet we sense right now and all around us<br>
Your ordering presence making manifest<br>
A greater age of glory to astound us<br>
With your all-wondrous love.<br>
And we are blessed<br>
To know that even while our earth is quaking,<br>
It is a better world that you are making.<br>
</p>
