## I. ARRIVAL OF ADAM AND EVE

1. The Adams and Eves are biologic uplifters. <a id="s28_46"></a>[UB 51:0.1](/en/The_Urantia_Book/51#p0_1)
2. Van had long proclaimed the arrival of a Son of God. <a id="s29_56"></a>[UB 73:2.1](/en/The_Urantia_Book/73#p2_1)
3. The secondary midwayers are indigenous to the Adamic missions. <a id="s30_66"></a>[UB 51:3.6](/en/The_Urantia_Book/51#p3_6)
4. Adam and Eve arrived on Urantia, from A.D. 1934, 37,848 years ago<a id="s31_68"></a>[UB 74:0.1](/en/The_Urantia_Book/74#p0_1)
5. The bodies of Adam and Eve gave forth a shimmering light. <a id="s32_61"></a>[UB 74:6.5](/en/The_Urantia_Book/74#p6_5)
6. Van proclaimed Adam and Eve rulers of Urantia. <a id="s33_50"></a>[UB 74:2.6](/en/The_Urantia_Book/74#p2_6)
7. Their arrival brought the end of a dispensation with resurrection of sleeping survivors. <a id="s34_92"></a>[UB 74:2.8](/en/The_Urantia_Book/74#p2_8)
8. Adam was confronted with confusion, degeneracy, and isolation—he had no one to advise him. <a id="s35_94"></a>[UB 75:1.1](/en/The_Urantia_Book/75#p1_1)

## II. THE GARDEN OF EDEN

1. The Garden provided homes and land for one million. <a id="s39_55"></a>[UB 73:5.1](/en/The_Urantia_Book/73#p5_1)
2. The Father’s Temple was at the center of the Garden. <a id="s40_56"></a>[UB 73:5.1](/en/The_Urantia_Book/73#p5_1)
3. The law of the Garden consisted of seven commandments:
	1. Health and sanitation.
	2. Social regulations.
	3. Trade and commerce.
	4. Fair play and competition.
	5. Home life.
	6. Golden rule.
	7. The seven moral rules. <a id="s48_27"></a>[UB 74:7.12](/en/The_Urantia_Book/74#p7_12)
4. The tree of life was real. <a id="s49_30"></a>[UB 73:6.3](/en/The_Urantia_Book/73#p6_3)

## III. DEFAULT OF ADAM AND EVE

1. Through flattery and persuasion Eve was led to compromise the divine plan. <a id="s53_78"></a>[UB 75:3.9](/en/The_Urantia_Book/75#p3_9)
2. The default consisted in the practice of good and evil. <a id="s54_59"></a>[UB 75:4.3](/en/The_Urantia_Book/75#p4_3)
3. Solonia informed the pair of their default. <a id="s55_47"></a>[UB 75:4.2](/en/The_Urantia_Book/75#p4_2)
4. Adam deliberately defaulted. He did not want to be separated from Eve. <a id="s56_74"></a>[UB 75:5.2](/en/The_Urantia_Book/75#p5_2)
5. Nothing is ever gained by attempting circumventing short cuts of the divine plan. <a id="s57_85"></a>[UB 75:8.5](/en/The_Urantia_Book/75#p8_5)

## IV. THE SECOND GARDEN

1. Adam and twelve hundred followers went forth to the second garden. <a id="s61_70"></a>[UB 75:6.2](/en/The_Urantia_Book/75#p6_2)
2. The pair learned that they were adjudged in default and had become human beings. <a id="s62_84"></a>[UB 75:7.3](/en/The_Urantia_Book/75#p7_3)
3. The default of Adam was not the “fall of man.” <a id="s63_50"></a>[UB 75:8.1](/en/The_Urantia_Book/75#p8_1)
4. Adam found the site of the second garden between the rivers vacated when he arrived. <a id="s64_88"></a>[UB 76:1.2](/en/The_Urantia_Book/76#p1_2)

## V. CAIN AND ABEL

1. Cain and Sansa were born enroute to the second garden. Laotta died. Eve adopted Sansa. <a id="s68_90"></a>[UB 76:0.2](/en/The_Urantia_Book/76#p0_2)
2. Abel became a herder; Cain a farmer. <a id="s69_40"></a>[UB 76:2.1](/en/The_Urantia_Book/76#p2_1)
3. Abel made animal offerings; Cain, the fruits of the field. <a id="s70_62"></a>[UB 76:2.2](/en/The_Urantia_Book/76#p2_2)
4. At ages eighteen and twenty, the brothers’ hatred reached the point where Cain killed Abel. <a id="s71_95"></a>[UB 76:2.5](/en/The_Urantia_Book/76#p2_5)
5. Cain fled to the land of Nod and married Remona. <a id="s72_52"></a>[UB 76:2.9](/en/The_Urantia_Book/76#p2_9)

## VI. THE VIOLET RACE

1. Adam and Eve were the founders of the violet race—the ninth human race. <a id="s76_75"></a>[UB 76:4.1](/en/The_Urantia_Book/76#p4_1)
2. Adam’s progeny were more resistant to disease than the other Urantian races. <a id="s77_80"></a>[UB 76:4.7](/en/The_Urantia_Book/76#p4_7)
3. The Adamites excelled in culture and produced the third alphabet. <a id="s78_69"></a>[UB 76:3.8](/en/The_Urantia_Book/76#p3_8)
4. Adam and Eve had 105 children, many of whom contributed to the Andite race. <a id="s79_79"></a>[UB 74:6.2](/en/The_Urantia_Book/74#p6_2)
5. Adam left 1,570 offspring of superior evolutionary races who also contributed to the founding of the mighty Andite race. <a id="s80_124"></a>[UB 76:4.8](/en/The_Urantia_Book/76#p4_8)

## VII. DEATH AND SURVIVAL OF ADAM AND EVE

1. Adam lived 530 years. Eve died nineteen years before Adam. <a id="s84_62"></a>[UB 76:5.5](/en/The_Urantia_Book/76#p5_5)
2. The third day after death, Adam and Eve were repersonalized with 1,316 of their associates, in special resurrection No. 26. <a id="s85_127"></a>[UB 76:6.2](/en/The_Urantia_Book/76#p6_2)
3. The Adamic acceleration of civilization was soon submerged. <a id="s86_63"></a>[UB 76:6.4](/en/The_Urantia_Book/76#p6_4)
4. It is the people who make a civilization; civilization does not make the people. <a id="s87_84"></a>[UB 76:6.4](/en/The_Urantia_Book/76#p6_4)
