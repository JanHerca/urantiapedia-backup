## I. THE NATURE OF PARADISE

1. The Universal Father revealed the infinity potential of his nonpersonal self as Paradise. <a id="s28_93"></a>[UB 11:9.3](/en/The_Urantia_Book/11#p9_3)
2. Paradise is not spherical—it is ellipsoid. <a id="s29_46"></a>[UB 11:2.2](/en/The_Urantia_Book/11#p2_2)
3. Time and space are nonexistent on Paradise. <a id="s30_47"></a>[UB 0:1.13](/en/The_Urantia_Book/0#p1_13)
4. Paradise is the center of the universe of universes. <a id="s31_56"></a>[UB 11:0.1](/en/The_Urantia_Book/11#p0_1)
5. Paradise is the geographic center of infinity. <a id="s32_50"></a>[UB 11:9.2](/en/The_Urantia_Book/11#p9_2)
6. Paradise is motionless—it is the Absolute of material-gravity control. <a id="s33_74"></a>[UB 0:4.12](/en/The_Urantia_Book/0#p4_12)
7. Paradise is composed of absolutum. <a id="s34_38"></a>[UB 11:2.9](/en/The_Urantia_Book/11#p2_9)
8. Paradise is the absolute of patterns. <a id="s35_41"></a>[UB 11:9.5](/en/The_Urantia_Book/11#p9_5)
9. Paradise is divided into three domains:
	> 1. Upper Paradise.
	> 2. Peripheral Paradise.
	> 3. Nether Paradise. <a id="s39_23"></a>[UB 11:2.4](/en/The_Urantia_Book/11#p2_4)
10. Paradise is not a creator—it is a unique controller. <a id="s40_57"></a>[UB 0:4.13](/en/The_Urantia_Book/0#p4_13)
11. Paradise is not Deity—it is not conscious. <a id="s41_47"></a>[UB 11:9.4](/en/The_Urantia_Book/11#p9_4)

## II. THE DIVINE RESIDENCE

1. Paradise is the dwelling place of the eternal God. <a id="s45_54"></a>[UB 0:0.5](/en/The_Urantia_Book/0#p0_5)
2. The Universal Father is cosmically focalized, spiritually personalized, and geographically resident on Paradise. <a id="s46_116"></a>[UB 11:1.2](/en/The_Urantia_Book/11#p1_2)

## III. UPPER PARADISE

1. Upper Paradise consists of three spheres:
	> 1. Deity presence.
	> 2. Most Holy Sphere.
	> 3. Holy Area. <a id="s53_17"></a>[UB 11:3.1](/en/The_Urantia_Book/11#p3_1)
2. Each of numerous residential units lodges one billion working groups. <a id="s54_73"></a>[UB 11:3.4](/en/The_Urantia_Book/11#p3_4)
3. Worship is the highest joy of Paradise residents. <a id="s55_53"></a>[UB 27:7.5](/en/The_Urantia_Book/27#p7_5)
4. Paradise is the destiny of all ascendant mortals. <a id="s56_53"></a>[UB 11:9.1](/en/The_Urantia_Book/11#p9_1)

## IV. PERIPHERAL PARADISE

1. On peripheral Paradise are the landing and dispatching fields; also the force-focal headquarters of the Seven Master Spirits. <a id="s60_129"></a>[UB 11:4.1](/en/The_Urantia_Book/11#p4_1)
2. Here are the historic and prophetic exhibit areas. <a id="s61_54"></a>[UB 11:4.3](/en/The_Urantia_Book/11#p4_3)

## V. NETHER PARADISE

1. On nether Paradise, all physical-energy circuits have their origin. <a id="s65_71"></a>[UB 11:5.1](/en/The_Urantia_Book/11#p5_1)
2. Here is the space presence of the Unqualified Absolute. <a id="s66_59"></a>[UB 11:5.7](/en/The_Urantia_Book/11#p5_7)
3. On nether Paradise is found the center for space respiration. <a id="s67_65"></a>[UB 11:5.5](/en/The_Urantia_Book/11#p5_5)
4. Paradise exists without time and has no location in space. <a id="s68_62"></a>[UB 11:2.10](/en/The_Urantia_Book/11#p2_10)
5. Space is a bestowal of Paradise. <a id="s69_36"></a>[UB 11:7.4](/en/The_Urantia_Book/11#p7_4)
