## I. GEOGRAPHY

1. Havona consists of one billion perfect worlds revolving around Paradise in seven concentric circuits. <a id="s28_105"></a>[UB 14:0.1](/en/The_Urantia_Book/14#p0_1)
2. Between Havona and Paradise are the three seven-world circuits of the Father, the Son, and the Spirit. <a id="s29_106"></a>[UB 13:0.1](/en/The_Urantia_Book/13#p0_1)
3. The dark islands of space lie between Havona and the seven superuniverses. <a id="s30_78"></a>[UB 14:1.12](/en/The_Urantia_Book/14#p1_12)
4. The Paradise-Havona system is a unit of creative perfection. <a id="s31_64"></a>[UB 14:2.6](/en/The_Urantia_Book/14#p2_6)

## II. THE HAVONA WORLDS

1. The Havona worlds consist of one thousand elements and seven forms of energy. <a id="s35_81"></a>[UB 14:2.3](/en/The_Urantia_Book/14#p5_6)
2. Havona natives are the children of the Trinity. <a id="s36_51"></a>[UB 14:4.10](/en/The_Urantia_Book/14#p4_10)
3. There is no government on the Havona worlds. <a id="s37_48"></a>[UB 14:3.2](/en/The_Urantia_Book/14#p3_2)
4. The standard time of the grand universe is the Paradise-Havona day-a little over seven minutes less than one thousand years of Urantia time. <a id="s38_144"></a>[UB 14:1.12](/en/The_Urantia_Book/14#p1_12)

## III. LIFE IN HAVONA

1. There are seven forms of life in Havona. <a id="s42_44"></a>[UB 14:4.1](/en/The_Urantia_Book/14#p4_1)
2. Sin has never appeared in Havona. <a id="s43_37"></a>[UB 14:2.9](/en/The_Urantia_Book/14#p2_9)
3. The Deities derive varied satisfactions from the perfect Havona universe. <a id="s44_77"></a>[UB 14:6.36](/en/The_Urantia_Book/14#p6_36)

## IV. THE SACRED SPHERES OF PARADISE

1. Each of the twenty-one worlds of the Deities is highly specialized. <a id="s48_71"></a>[UB 13:0.7](/en/The_Urantia_Book/13#p0_7)
2. The worlds of the Father are directed by the Trinitized Secrets of Supremacy. <a id="s49_81"></a>[UB 13:1.2](/en/The_Urantia_Book/13#p1_2)
3. The worlds of the Father are:
	1. Divinington.
	2. Sonarington.
	3. Spiritington.
	4. Vicegerington.
	5. Solitarington.
	6. Seraphington.
	7. Ascendington. <a id="s57_18"></a>[UB 13:1.4](/en/The_Urantia_Book/13#p1_4)
4. The seven worlds of the Son are the home of the seven phases of pure-spirit existence. <a id="s58_90"></a>[UB 13:3.1](/en/The_Urantia_Book/13#p3_1)
5. The seven worlds of the Spirit are the headquarters of the Seven Master Spirits. From these worlds the cosmic-mind circuits to the grand universe are equalized and stabilized. <a id="s59_179"></a>[UB 13:4.6](/en/The_Urantia_Book/13#p4_6)
