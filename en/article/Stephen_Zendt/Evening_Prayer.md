<p style="text-align:center;">
God who makes the angels bright<br>
<br>
God who guards the darkest night<br>
<br>
I am your child<br>
<br>
I need your love<br>
<br>
My fears relieve!<br>
<br>
My thoughts above make moments tender<br>
<br>
Focus my faith so I remember<br>
that as I seek so shall I find<br>
<br>
Come, cure the wild and aimless mind<br>
<br>
Cast out the doubt of heavenly might<br>
And keep me always in your sight<br>
<br>
Forever lead me into light.<br>
</p>
