We would like to hear your feedback and user experience so that we can best proceed with the development and improvement of the Graduation Memorial Page.

Please submit details to cristina.seaborn@urantiabook,org

(Cristina Seaborn) of a loved one you would like to see included here. Please send a photo, and a paragraph or more about the person's life.

Graduation Memorial Page https://urantiabook.org/graduation-memorial

In addition, please comment on the Graduation Memorial Forum. Anybody can leave a story about a graduate here: https://urantiabook.org/GraduationMemorial-Forum

Please send a name with a photo and story you would like to see here to: cristina.seaborn7@gmail.com

Thank you for your help! We can't do this alone!

Cristina Seaborn, Joshua Wilson, Jena Lassiter, and Richard Daunt
