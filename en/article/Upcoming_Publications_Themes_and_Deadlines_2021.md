Calling all Urantia authors, poets, videographers, musicians, and artists!

We have a monthly e-newsletter the Mini Messenger - deadline the 10th of each month (open to Urantia themes).

The summer Fellowship Herald theme is the Spirit of Truth and the Holy Spirit - deadline April 10th 2021.

The fall Mighty Messenger theme is Stepping Stones to the Revelation. This is for an individual who is a truth seeker who doesn't know the Urantia book - deadline September 10th 2021.

We have the following promotion policy if your article is asking for support for your project:

Project Promotion Policy

_The Urantia Book_ Fellowship (hereinafter the Fellowship) will use its resources including its publications, database (including mailing addresses, email addresses and website), personnel, and all other resources to promote, recommend, and solicit support (financial and other) for projects and efforts under the direct jurisdiction of the Fellowship, including efforts of societies.

Other organizations supporting _The Urantia Book_ may have their projects supported with approval from the Executive Committee. The Fellowship is interested in supporting Urantia Book based efforts.

Approved by the General Council 07/06/2008

Review ed by the Policy and Procedure Ad-hoc Committee 06/19/2015

Revised by the Executive Committee 09/17/2020

Fellowship's Project Promotion Policy
