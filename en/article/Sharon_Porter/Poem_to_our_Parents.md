<p style="text-align:center;">
I don’t need to wait ‘til Father’s Day to tell you my Father, Creator Michael, to say, <br>
I love you Father <br>
Buying you a gift our dear Divine Mother would be in excess, <br>
just another fad, to say to you I love you Mother, <br>
my Creator, would be to tell the truth <br>
Thank you dear Divine Parents for being my mom and dad <br>
for being my real parents <br>
for creating me, <br>
loving me, nurturing and teaching me <br>
Oh, what Majestic Beauties You Are! <br>
Thank You <br>
</p>
