It has been a personal goal of Bill Rocap's to see the completion of the translation of the Urantia Book into the Turkish language. Being so blessed to live in Turkey, to be immersed in such rich history and culture, to share life with these friendly people, Bill felt he must “pay it forward” with the translation of his favorite spiritual book.

_The Urantia Book_ Society of Greater New York sponsored the translation process and has provided the vital money and assistance to see this goal completed. To see the Turkish translation of the Urantia Book go to:

https://www.facebook.com/urantiabookinturkish
