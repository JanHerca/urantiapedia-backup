1 And God said to his only begotten Son, “Go, take the soul of my beloved Sedrach, and put it in Paradise.”

2 The only begotten Son said to Sedrach, “Give me that which our Father deposited in the womb of your mother in your holy dwelling place since you were born.”

3 Sedrach said, “I will not give you my soul.”

4 God said to him, “And why was I sent, and why did I come here, and you make a pretense? to me?

5 I was commanded by my father not to hesitate in taking your soul; hence, give me your most desired soul.”
