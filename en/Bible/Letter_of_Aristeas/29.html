Version of Chapter 10 in Version 1

1 And after Abraham had seen the place of judgment, the cloud took him down upon the firmament below,

2 and Abraham, looking down upon the earth, saw a man committing adultery with a wedded woman.

3 And Abraham turning said to Michael, “Do you see this wickedness? But, Lord, send fire from heaven to consume them.”

4 And straightway there came down fire and consumed them,

5 for the Lord had said to Michael, do whatever Abraham shall ask you to do for him.

6 Abraham looked again, and saw other men railing at their companions,

7 and said, “Let the earth open and swallow them,”

8 and as he spoke the earth swallowed them alive.

9 Again the cloud led him to another place, and Abraham saw some going into a desert place to commit murder,

10 and he said to Michael, “Do you see this wickedness? But let wild beasts come out of the desert, and tear them in pieces,”

11 and that same hour wild beasts came out of the desert, and devoured them.

12 Then the Lord God spoke to Michael saying, “Turn away Abraham to his own house, and let him not go round all the creation that I have made, because he has no compassion on sinners,

13 but I have compassion on sinners that they may turn and live, and repent of their sins and be saved.”
