Ezra struggles for his soul

3 Then a voice came to me, “Come here, die, Ezra, my beloved! Give back that

4 which has been entrusted (to you).” And the prophet said, “And whence can

5 you bring forth my soul?” And the angels said, “We can cast it forth through

6 your mouth.” And the prophet said, “I spoke mouth to mouth with God and it

7 will not go forth from there.” And the angels said. “We will bring it forth through

8 your nostrils.” And the prophet said, “My nostrils smelled the glory of God.”"

16 cannot receive his soul.” Then he said to his only begotten son, “Go down, my beloved son, with a numerous host of angels, taking the soul of my beloved Ezra.”

17 For the Lord, having taken a numerous army of many angels, said to the prophet, “Give me that deposit which I entrusted to you. The crown is readied for you.”

18 And the prophet said, “Lord, if you take my soul from me, who will you have

19 left to plead on behalf of the race of men?” And God said, “You who are mortal

20 and earthly, do not plead the case with me.” And the prophet said, “I shall never

21 cease pleading.” And God said, “Give, in the meanwhile, that which is entrusted

22 (to you). The crown is readied for you. Come here, die, so that you may attain

23 it.” Then the prophet began to speak with tears, “O Lord, what profits it that I

24 plead the case with you, and I am going to fall to the earth? Woe, woe! for I will

25 be consumed by worms. Bewail me, all holy and pious ones, I plead greatly and

26 am delivered over to death! Bewail me, all holy and just ones, because I have entered the bowl of Hades.”
