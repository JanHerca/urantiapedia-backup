1 And I Baruch said to the angel,

2 Let me ask thee one thing, Lord. Since thou didst say to me

3 that the dragon drinks one cubit out of the sea, say to me also, how great is his belly And the angel said, His belly is Hades; and as far as a plummet is thrown (by) three hundred men, so great is his belly. Come, then, that I may show thee also greater works than these.
