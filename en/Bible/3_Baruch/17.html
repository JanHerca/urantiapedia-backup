1 And while he yet spake, the door was closed, and we withdrew.

2 And the angel took me and

3 restored me to the place where I was at the beginning. And having come to myself, I gave glory

4 to God, who counted me worthy of such honor. Wherefore do ye also, brethren, who obtained such a revelation, yourselves also glorify God, so that He also may glorify you, now and ever, and to all eternity. Amen.
