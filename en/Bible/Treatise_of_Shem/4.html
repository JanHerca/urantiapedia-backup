1 And if the year begins in Cancer: In the beginning of the year there will be a sufficiency of produce and people will be healthy.

2 And the Nile will overflow half its (usual) rate.

3 And Alexandria will be afflicted, and misery from the plague will be in it.

4 And the stars will shine magnificently for the moon will be eclipsed.

5 And many ships will be wrecked in the sea.

6 And in the beginning of the ye[ar wheat and barley will be expensive. ]

7 And winds will increase, then many people will be ill from sties (of the eyes) and from coughing and vomiting.

8 And wine will be abundant, but bulls, and sheep, and small cattle will perish, even dried peas will perish.

9 But oil will compensate (for) them.

10 Then at the end of the year the harvest will be wearisome for nine days, but afterward there will be rain.

11 And great blessing will be in (this year).
