1 And if the year begins in Libra: There will be spring rains.

2 And the year will be transformed.

3 And people will be spared from the east wind.

4 And fig trees will not produce fruit.

5 But dates and oil will be plentiful.

6 But wine will be expensive.

7 And wheat will be valued greatly. And the locust: will appear.

8 And a severe war will occur in Africa.

9 And men will have severe diseases.

10 And in the middle of the year rain will be held back (for) twenty days.

11 And cultivated wheat will not ripent well.

12 And all lands will be good.

13 And everyone whose name has a Yūdh or Béth will be sick, and he will have anxiety, and will go into exile from his land.

14 And wine will be damaged.

15 And adultery will increase, and (licentious) desire will increase.

16 And the king will stay in one place.

17 And power will leave the land.

18 And the nobles will flee to the sea, and there will be between (them) [in] the {sea} a severe war.

19 And there will be in Galilee a severe earthquake.

20 And robbers will gather in Hauran and in Damascus.

21 And the river Nile will overflow (at) its highest rate.

22 And a severe plague will occur in Egypt, and it will be in [Gali]lee? as in Beth Bardune (the Place of Mules?).

23 People will be troubled because of (the lack of) rain.
