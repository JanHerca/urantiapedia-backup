1 And if the year begins in Virgo: Everyone whose name contain Yūdhs or Semkath, and Béth, and Nun will be diseased and robbed, and will flee from his home.

2 And (this misfortune) will occur in the beginning of the year.

3 And shortage of water will be in every circle.

4 And the. first grain will not prosper.

5 And people will suffer (many) miseries: in winter and summer.

6 But the last grain will be harvested and it will be good.

7 And produce will be expensive in Hauran and Bithynia, but at the end of the year it: will be inexpensive.

8 Even wine will be less (expensive) and pleasant.

9 And dates will be abundant.

10 But oil will be expensive.

11 And wheat and barley will be valued, but dried pea(s) will be reduced in value.

12 And rain will be late and will not fall upon the earth until? thirty days before the Passover [feast].

13 And the king will strive with a king and will slay him.

14 And Alexandria will be lost.

15 And [the Nile] will not overflow well.

16 And many ships will be wrecked.

17 But at the end of the year there will be a sufficiency about everything .
