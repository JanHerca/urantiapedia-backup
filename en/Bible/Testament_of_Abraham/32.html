1 And Abraham said, “My Lord, I beseech you to lead me to the place of judgment so that I too may see how they are judged.”

2 Then Michael took Abraham upon a cloud, and led him into Paradise,

3 and when he came to the place where the judge was, the angel came and gave that soul to the judge.

4 And the soul said, “Lord have mercy on me.”

5 And the judge said, “How shall I have mercy upon you, when you had no mercy upon your daughter which you had, the fruit of your womb? Wherefore did you slay her?”

6 It answered, “Nay, Lord, slaughter has not been done by me, but my daughter has lied upon me.”

7 But the judge commanded him to come that wrote down the records,

8 and behold cherubim carrying two books. And there was with them a man of exceeding great stature, having on his head three crowns,

9 and the one crown was higher than the other two. These are called the crowns of witness.

10 And the man had in his hand a golden pen, and the judge said to him, Exhibit the sin of this soul.

11 And that man, opening one of the books of the cherubim, sought out the sin of the woman's soul and found it.

12 And the judge said, “O wretched soul, why do you say that you have not done murder?

13 Did you not, after the death of your husband, go and commit adultery with your daughter's husband, and kill her?”

14 And he convicted her also of her other sins, whatsoever she had done from her youth.

15 Hearing these things the woman cried out, saying, “Woe is me, all the sins that I did in the world I forgot, but here they were not forgotten.”

16 Then they took her away also and gave her over to the tormentors.
