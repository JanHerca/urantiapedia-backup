## Milestone I

For each language supported the list of tasks to complete milestone I is this:
1. Translation of *Paramony* from English to the target language (`Paramony`).
2. Translation and review of *Topic index* from English to the target language (`Topics`).
3. Obtain a public-domain translation of *The Bible* in the target language (`Bible`).
4. Conversion *The Urantia Book* from HTML to JSON without footnotes (`UB`).
5. Addition of footnotes to *The Urantia Book* in JSON (`UB + footnotes`).
6. Conversion of the *Topic index* from TXT to Wiki.js (`Topics Wiki`).
7. Conversion of *The Urantia Book* from JSON with footnotes to Wiki.js including references to topics (`UB Wiki`).
8. Conversion of *The Bible* from LaTeX to Wiki.js with footnotes (`Bible Wiki`).
9. Commit changes to [Urantiapedia-backup](https://github.com/JanHerca/urantiapedia-backup) project (`Commit`).
