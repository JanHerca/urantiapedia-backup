The Designers and Embellishers are a group belonging to the Celestial Artisans who are organized into the following corps:

  1. Craftworkers of color. [1]
  2. Sound designers. [2]
  3. Emotion designers. [3]
  4. Artists of color. [4]
  5. Presence embellishers. [5]
  6. Taste designers. [6]
  7. Morontia synthesizers. [7]
