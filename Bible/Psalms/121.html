Psalms - 121

1 I will lift up mine eyes unto the mountains: From whence shall my help come? [1]

2 My help [cometh] from Jehovah, Who made heaven and earth. [2]

3 He will not suffer thy foot to be moved: He that keepeth thee will not slumber.

4 Behold, he that keepeth Israel Will neither slumber nor sleep. [3]

5 Jehovah is thy keeper: Jehovah is thy shade upon thy right hand.

6 The sun shall not smite thee by day, Nor the moon by night.

7 Jehovah will keep thee from all evil; He will keep thy soul.

8 Jehovah will keep thy going out and thy coming in From this time forth and for evermore. [4]
