Univitatia

See also: UB 43:7.

Administer constellation morontia worlds. [1]Ascending mortals live with univitatia on Edentia. [2]Children of Creator Son and Mother Spirit. [3]Do not function beyond constellation headquarters. [4]In personality registers. [5]Known for artistic skill and social adaptability. [6]Largest group associated with celestial artisans. [7]Learning to abide joyfully with. [8]Nonreproducing beings existing on plane between Material Sons and Spirit-fused mortals; 70 different forms. [9]Permanent population of constellation headquarters worlds. [10]Possible future citizens of Havona. [11]
