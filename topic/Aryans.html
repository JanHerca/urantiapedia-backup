Aryans

33 deities. [1]

Andite Vedic conquerors of India ca. 2500 B.C.; from Turkestan

See also: UB 78:5.6; UB 79:4; UB 94:1.1.

Little racial impression except in north India. [2]Met undoing in failure to complete conquest of India. [3]Used caste system to save racial identity. [4]

Andites were pre-Aryan. [5]Hellenic invaders of Greece; destroyed Salem teachings. [6]Language of Aryans gave similarity to Occidental tongues. [7]Migration into Levant and India. [8]Semidemocratic. [9]Sumerian language had much in common with Aryan. [10]
