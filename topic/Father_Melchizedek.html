Father Melchizedek

See also: UB 35:1; UB 189:1.10.

Original Melchizedek Son. [1]Executive associate of Gabriel; chief executive in Gabriel’s. Absence. [2]Never away from Salvington when Gabriel is away. [3]Always free to travel in interests of his order. [4]Created Melchizedek Sons with Creator Son and Mother Spirit. [5]Created spironga with Gabriel. [6]Directs stabilization corps in light and life. [7]Order in Paradise classification. [8]Presides over emergency commissions, advisory bodies. [9]Represents Michael in Life Carrier deliberations. [10]Temple to Michael is in foreground of F.M.’s home. [11]With Jesus at transfiguration. [12]
