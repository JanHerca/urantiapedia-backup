Greeks

Backbone of early Christian church. [1]Carried Christianity to whole Roman Empire. [2]Contrasted with Jews and Romans. [3]Favored monogamous marriage. [4]Great teachers, artists, traders, and colonizers. [5]

Individual Greeks

Alexander the Great q.v. Anaxagoras. [6]Aristotle. [7]Contractor in Corinth. [8]Inquiring Greeks q.v. Mistress of inn in Corinth. [9]Philosopher in Athens. [10]Philosopher in Ramah. [11]Physician in Rome. [12]Plato. [13]Rodan of Alexandria q.v. Sato, leader of Adamsonite settlers. [14]
