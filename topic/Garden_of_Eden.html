Edenites

1200 loyal Edenites at time of default. [6] Cooperation from Nodites. [7] Education. [8] Enter Mesopotamia. [9] Had no suitable boats. [10] Ideal of whole family as gardeners. [11] Language was Andonic dialect. [12] Massacred nearby Nodites upon learning of Eve’s folly with Cano. [13] Practiced scrupulous burial of all waste. [14] Primitive manufacturing. [15]

Garden established; existing residents evicted. [16] Life in Garden of Eden far from Utopian. [17] Named after Edentia. [18] No animals ever slaughtered in. [19] Occupied by tribes after default. [20]

Physical description

27 mile wide neck connected to mainland. [21] 12,000 miles of paved roads. [22] Area between walls occupied by wild beasts. [23] Brick walls across neck of peninsula. [24] Elevated Mediterranean peninsula; salubrious climate. [25] Hills abounded in precious stones and metals. [26] Inaugural mount. [27] Most beautiful spot on earth. [28] Sanitary practices.
