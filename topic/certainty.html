Certainty

Convictions of religious experience are unassailable. [1]Does not fear outward analysis. [2]Fetishmen believed to be infallible. [3]God more real than our fellows. [4]In science, philosophy, and religious experience. [5]Let pressure develop certainty. [6]Of the God-knowing religionist. [7]On spiritual level, need for proof vanishes before experience with reality. [8]Some religionists believe they have the infallible Truth. [9]

Unattainable in mortal state

See also: UB 103:7.10.

Extends only as far as one’s personal experience. [10]None in knowledge, only increasing probability. [11]Nothing which human nature touches is infallible. [12]

We have right to be most certain about God. [13]
