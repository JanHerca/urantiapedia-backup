Mary (sister of Lazarus)

See also: UB 127:3.5.

2 years younger than Jesus. [1]At Jesus’ 4th appearance. [2]At resurrection of Lazarus. [3]Believed Jesus’ warning that he must die. [4]Chose good and needful part. [5]Disposed of possessions, joined Lazarus at Philadelphia. [6]First met Jesus. [7]Jesus and apostles stayed with. [8]Jesus loved Mary with fervent affection. [9]Mary loved Jesus like a brother. [10]Poured ointment on Jesus’ head and feet; Judas resented waste. [11]Wealthy; inherited extensive vineyards and olive orchards. [12]
