The banner of Michael, the material emblem of the Trinity government of all creation, is the three azure blue concentric circles on a white background. [1] The three circles are emblematic of the infinity, eternity, and universality of the Paradise Trinity. [2] Melchizedek wore it on his chest as an emblem. [3]

The triads that grew up in many religions all were reminiscent of the trinity teachings of the Andites and the Sumerians and based on the belief of the Salemites in Melchizedek’s insignia of the three circles. [4]
