Creature-trinitized sons

See also: UB 22:8.

All endowed with personality by Father. [1]Apprentices of Trinity Teacher Sons. [2]Come to worlds in light and life. [3]Command is to be understanding. [4]Conceptual potential mobilization. [5]Destiny is Corps of Trinitized Finaliters. [6]Do not grow as do ascenders. [7]Embody ideals and experience pertaining to future age. [8]Gather on 1st circuit of Havona. [9]Headquarters in Satania is 5th transition world. [10]In personality register. [11]Initial training and service under Seven Supreme Executives. [12]Mixed liaison types become wards of Architects of Master Universe; go to Vicegerington in company of Solitary Messengers and never leave. [13]Number 9642 in Nebadon. [14]Primary and secondary supernaphim are custodians. [15]Progeny of finaliters and Paradise-Havoners. [16]
