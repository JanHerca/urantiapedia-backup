Geologic ages

1. Archeozoic

See also: UB prelife era.

1 billion to 550 million years ago. [1]

2. Proterozoic

See also: UB life-dawn era.

550 to 400 million years ago. [2]

See also: UB marine-life era.

400 to 150 million years ago. [3]

Cambrian

See also: UB trilobite age.

400 to 350 million years ago. [4]

Ordovician

See also: UB invertebrate-animal age.

350 to 300 million years ago. [5]

Silurian

300 to 275 million years ago. [6]

Devonian

270 to 220 million years ago. [7]

220 to 180 million years ago. [8]

Permian

See also: UB seed-plant age; biologic tribulation.

170 to 150 million years ago. [9]

See also: UB early land-life era.

150 to 50 million years ago. [10]

Triassic

150 to 125 million years ago. [11]

Jurassic

See also: UB later-reptilian age.

125 to 100 million years ago. [12]

100 to 50 million years ago. [13]

50 to 35 million years ago. [15]

See also: UB advanced mammal age.

35 to 25 million years ago. [16]

25 to 10 million years ago. [17]
