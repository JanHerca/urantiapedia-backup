Descendants of lemurs. [1] Fear led to wise precautionary measures. [2] Gave rise to mid-mammals after 70 generations. [3] Killed off by mid-mammals. [4] Lemurlike mother escaped death by mere hairbreadth. [5] Possessed large brains; walked mostly on hind feet. [6] Shared instincts with primitive man. [7] Sudden appearance 1 million years ago. [8] Tribal spirit; pugnacious when disturbed. [9]
