Jesus of Nazareth’ earth life did not constitute an example for us, nor for any subsequent generation on Urantia or on any other world. Rather his life in the flesh was the inspiration for all lives upon all Nebadon worlds. [1] Children are permanently impressed only by the loyalties of their adult associates; precept or even example is not lastingly influential. [2]
