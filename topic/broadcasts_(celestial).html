Broadcasts (celestial)

See also: UB 46:3.

Adjusters receive broadcasts continuously via master circuits. [1]Back registry insures proper reception. [2]Extended to all worlds except for those quarantined. [3]Havona pilgrims consult broadcasts for news on friends’ arrivals. [4]John saw vision of broadcasts on Salvington. [5]Melodies can be broadcast to entire universe.
