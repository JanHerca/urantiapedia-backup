Trinity Teacher Sons

See also: UB 19:1; UB 20:7-9.

21 billion in service; Paradise reserves; numbers increasing. [1]15,000 to 20,000 in Nebadon. [2]Created by Trinity. [3]Creature-trinitized sons are apprentices of. [4]Do not incarnate; contact worlds through Brilliant Evening Stars. [5]Exist in several orders. [6]In personality registers. [7]Magisterial Sons accompany Trinity Teacher Sons to terminate dispensations. [8]Michael was private secretary to a Trinity Teacher Sons on seraphic bestowal. [9]No personal spiritual drawing power. [10]No preliminary training. [11]Paradise Sons of God; order of Daynals. [12]Residence in Jerusem circles. [13]

Service

Administer examinations for all phases of service. [14]Advised Melchizedek receivers after rebellion. [15]Angels partake of circulating teachings of. [16]Arrive after planet achieves spiritual development. [17]As local universe ambassadors. [18]Assigned to supreme council of unlimited authority in local universe light and life. [19]Come in groups of 71 to prepare planets for light and life; not materialized; remain at least 1000 years. [20]Completely identified with mortal progression. [21]Concerned with moral enlightenment, spiritual development. [22]Conscious teachers of all personalities. [23]Counsel Planetary Sovereign and finaliters in light and life. [24]Inaugurate light and life, morontia temple on terminal mission. [25]Make real spiritual counterparts to temporal wisdom. [26]On spheres circling Salvington. [27]One rules a Havona sphere when its Eternal of Days is absent. [28]Personal representatives of Father in local systems. [29]Release midwayers from planets in light and life. [30]Return many times to a world. [31]Sponsor Nebadon educational system. [32]Successive planetary missions in light and life. [33]Supreme Trinity co-ordinating personalities. [34]Under Creator Sons in local universes. [35]Work closely with finaliters. [36]

Visit Urantia. [37]
