Majeston

See also: UB 30:1.109.

50th Reflective Spirit. [1]Actualized by Deity Absolute in response to Supreme Being. [2]Created by Trinity and Seven Master Spirits. [3]Creation of Majeston only instance of Supreme acting directly as creator. [4]Focalizes presence of Supreme Mind. [5]Infallible center and chief of reflectivity. [6]Is a true person. [7]Nominates Paradise Companions. [8]Presides over millennial conclaves. [9]Reflectivator Liaisons. [10]Reflective Image Aids represent. [11]Synthetic co-ordinator of all creature-Creator activities.
