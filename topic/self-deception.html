Self-deception

Greatest self-deception is seeking to deprive others of natural liberties. [1]Leads to fears, enslaving pleasures, envy, and hatred. [2]Logic largely obscured by self-interest. [3]Lucifer persuaded himself rebellion was good for system. [4]One’s mind often justifies continuance in error. [5]Pleasant adjustment to paths of sin. [6]
