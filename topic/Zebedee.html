Zebedee

See also: UB 137:3.5; UB 138:5.4; UB 140:7.3.

Celebrated Passover with Annas in Jerusalem. [1]Directed son John buy house with Jesus’ money. [2]Family almost worshiped Jesus. [3]Father-partner of Jesus. [4]Home served as headquarters for kingdom. [5]In Capernaum, Jesus made his home with. [6]Jesus joined Zebedee’s boatmaking business. [7]Jesus told Zebedee about upcoming Mediterranean journey. [8]Owned fish-drying business. [9]Purchased Joseph’s equity in a Capernaum property. [10]With wife Salome had 3 sons, 4 daughters. [11]
