Volcanoes

See also: UB 60:3.16; UB 61:1.11-12; UB 61:2.3; UB 61:3.7.

Age of volcanoes from 1.5 to 1 billion years ago. [1]At Montreal. [2]Became fetishes. [3]Extensive volcanoes at end of continental drift. [4]Lava cools into granite. [5]Lava diversified earth’s crust; insulated planet from space-energies. [6]Lava equalized internal-heat pressure with crustal contraction. [7]Lava flows brought heavy metals to surface. [8]Volcanoes of eastern Kentucky was one of greatest. [9]Yahweh associated with Mount Horeb volcano. [10]
