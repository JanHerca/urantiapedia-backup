Amos (Prophet)

Denounced drunkenness, immorality of northern tribes. [1]Father spoke through. [2]John the Baptist came from herdsmen like. [3]Proclaimed Yahweh God of all nations; attacked chosen people concept. [4]Secret writing began Old Testament. [5]Threatened God would abandon Israel unless righteousness reestablished. [6]Stoned to death. [7]
