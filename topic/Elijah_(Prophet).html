Elijah (Prophet)

See also: UB 135:6.3.

Restored concept of God to northern kingdom. [1]Prophet of Mount Carmel. [2]Secluded himself. [3]Denounced Ahab for murder of Naboths; overthrew altars of Baal; demolished idols. [4]Father spoke through. [5]Made Yahweh into Elohim. [6]Shifted Yahweh-Baal controversy from land to religion. [7]One of 24 counselors. [8]Peter believed he saw Elijah at transfiguration. [9]Secret writing began Old Testament. [10]Translated soul of brilliant spiritual achievement. [11]

John the Baptist

Adopted dress and method of Elijah. [12]Denied he was Elijah. [13]Held back 2 years by expectation of return of. [14]Jesus referred to John as Elijah. [15]
