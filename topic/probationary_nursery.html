Probationary nursery

See also: UB 47:2; UB 49:6.11-14.

Children in

Absent choice for ascendant career, die at life’s end; no. Adjudication. [1]Adjusterless children enter probationary nursery upon survival of parent of most advanced spiritual status. [2]Adjusters indwell. [3]Are wards of the finaliters. [4]Attended by seraphic guardians, spornagia. [5]Cannot become finaliters. [6]Constitute permanent ascendant citizenship of Paradise. [7]Given opportunity to choose heavenly way. [8]Personal guardians serve those attaining 3rd circle. [9]Raised in families of 5. [10]Repersonalized as of status at death except no sex differentiation. [11]

Children who die after receiving Adjusters but before the age of spiritual choice live with Material Sons; do not go to probationary nursery. [12]Devoted to children who die before acquiring individual status in event of survival of either parent. [13]Half of finaliters’ world devoted to. [14]Lucifer and Satan sought to corrupt. [15]Similar nurseries also maintained on constellations and local universe headquarters. [16]Spironga minister to physical necessities. [17]Supervised by 1000 Material Son and Daughter couples. [18]Survivors deficient in parental experience must qualify in probationary nursery, possibly with own children. [19]Visitation by mortal parents. [20]
