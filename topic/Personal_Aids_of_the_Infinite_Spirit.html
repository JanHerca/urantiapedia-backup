Personal Aids of the Infinite Spirit

See also: UB 24:3.

Exist to execute bidding of Infinite Spirit. [1]In personality registers. [2]Located on Paradise; flash to uttermost parts of creation. [3]Mortals will not encounter. [4]No presence-form; difficult to regard as persons. [5]Number in trillions. [6]Paradise-origin beings aware of presence of. [7]Traverse space like Solitary Messengers. [8]Tremendous endowment of antigravity. [9]
