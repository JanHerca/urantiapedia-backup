Atenas was the cultural center of Alexander’s empire. [1] Jesus held a talk on science with a Greek philosopher in Athens. [2]

The Hellenization of Christianity started in earnest on that eventful day when the Apostle Paul stood before the council of the Areopagus in Athens and told the Athenians about “the Unknown God”. [3]
