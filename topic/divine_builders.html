Divine builders are one of groups in celestial artisans.

Types

The types of divine builders are: [1]

  1. The home designers and builders.
  2. The vocation builders.
  3. The play builders.
  4. The worship builders.
  5. The education builders.
  6. Morontia planners.
  7. The public builders.

All the buildings they build are not visible to mortals, but they are very real and are discernible by supermaterial beings.
