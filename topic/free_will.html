Free will

Adjuster, future finaliter, Father, and Supreme depend on our decisions. [1]Adjusters may indwell beings who have free will. [2]Cannot choose beyond range of choosable. [3]Dedication of free will is man’s choicest gift to God. [4]

Defined as

Ability to know God and power to worship him; to choose. Eternal survival. [5]Absolutely sovereign regarding eternal destiny. [6]Dangerous, unstable unidentified with spirit. [7]Determining factor in human experience. [8]Empowered to rescind spiritual choice and reject survival at any time before fusion. [9]Includes possibility of self-confusion, self-disruption, and self-destruction. [10]Increasingly liberated as universes are ascended. [11]Maximated decision; deliberate choice leading to decision-conduct. [12]Mind arena of choice. [13]Not unrestricted, but respected in spirit world provided choices not injurious to self or others. [14]Range of choice necessary to enjoy universe progression by making contact with reality. [15]Relative; determines actions. [16]Residing in material mind circuits in mortal life. [17]

Early tribulation resulting from liberty offset by later higher loyalty. [18]Enables subjective consciousness to be Godlike. [19]Endowing imperfect beings with free will entails inevitable tragedy. [20]Endowment of personalities. [21]Father has ordained free will; no personality has power or authority to curtail or abridge. [22]Father’s gift of personality makes mortals self-determinative and self-creative. [23]Fixed policy of universe law not to infringe. [24]

Historically

Decision of twins to flee was first act of. [25]Development of speech usually coincides with acquiring. [26]Edentia authorities would not intervene in rebellion until every person made final decision. [27]Life Carriers may not arbitrarily influence. [28]

[33]Mortals are subjects of predestination, but may reject any part. [34]Mortals may draw near and forsake God so long as power of choice remains. [41]That creature can do wrong establishes fact of free will. [42]
