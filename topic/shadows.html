Shadows

Cease to be attracted by unreal and material shadows. [1]Feared and revered by early man. [2]Follow substance. [3]Interpret in terms of substance. [4]Matter is shadow cast by mind in presence of spirit. [5]Once “walled” into new buildings. [6]Shadow of hair’s turning for untrue purpose. [7]Unrobust souls had shorter shadows. [8]
