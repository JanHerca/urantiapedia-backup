Balance

In Judaism, philosophy and aesthetics entirely submerged in religion and morality. [1]Lack of balance between intellectual and spiritual growth hazardous. [2]Lack of balance in Greece, where personal religious experience failed to keep pace with intellect. [3]Philosophy, religion, and science welded together by wisdom, faith, and experience. [4]Sense of proportion necessary in exercise of virtue. [5]Spiritual growth greatest when physical, mental, and spiritual powers in balance. [6]Unification of mathematic logic, mind-reason, and spirit-faith in personality. [7]
