Horses

Appearance. [1]Extinct in America before arrival of red man. [2]In general use by 5000 B.C. [3]Intelligence approaches that of elephant. [4]Stealing horses once capital offense. [5]Tend to flee when frightened. [6]

Value

Andites of Turkestan first extensively domesticated. [7]Determined dominance of Andites in Occident. [8]For food, travel, agriculture, war. [9]Hunted by Neanderthalers. [10]Not domesticated in days of Prince’s staff. [11]Offered barbarians tremendous military advantage. [12]

Vishnu shown with horse’s head. [13]
