Corps of Trinitized Finaliters is one of the seven finaliter corps. [1]

Excepting the Trinitized Sons of Perfection and those who are forgathering on Vicegerington, the supreme destiny of all creature-trinitized sons and certain Paradise Citizens appears to be entrance into the Corps of Trinitized Finaliters. [2] They appear in Uversa personality register. [3]
