Cain

See also: UB 76:2.

Son of Eve and Cano; natural bellicose inheritance. [1]Born before Adamic caravan reached second garden. [2]2 years older than Able; followed agriculture. [3]Always defiant of family discipline and religion. [4]Slew Abel. [5]Repented, was indwelt by Adjuster. [6]Encouraged to leave second garden. [7]Went to Nod for wife. [8]Married Remona; first son was Enoch. [9]Avenged 7 times. [10]Tribal mark vital in days of. [11]
