Finaliters

Acting planetary chief of finaliters has seat in morontia temple. [1]Alternating periods of duty and free service. [2]Before spirit vision, finaliters made visible to survivors by energy transformers, Morontia Power Supervisors. [3]Come to know the Supreme by experience. [4]

Destiny

As 7th stage spirits. Finaliters become 7th stage spirits, with attainment of the Supreme. [5]Finaliters have hitherto unattained wisdom. [6]Finaliters’s minds unify with then-actualized Supreme. [7]Group attainment of ultimate status. [8]May give up residential status on Paradise. [9]Attain destiny with Trinity embrace. [10]Explore infinity of Universal Father. [11]May fully comprehend time-space divinity of God the Sevenfold. [12]May join Mighty Messengers. [13]May join Those High in Authority. [14]May join Those without Name and Number. [15]May realize supremacy and ultimacy of God. [16]Speculations concerning. [17]To administer outer space universes. [18]Unknown future service with Creator Sons. [19]Will be superb rulers, most effective of all administrators. [20]

Finality oath administered individually by head of Architects of Master Universe. [21]Finality oath to Trinity. [22]First acknowledge extra-Paradise authority in local universe light and life. [23]Granted clearance to Sonarington. [24]Invisible to mortals except in morontia temple. [25]Midsoniters are wards of. [26]Midwayers are contact personalities for finaliters in light and life. [27]

Nature of finaliters

6th stage spirits; status not final. [28]Children of the Supreme. [29]Each stamped with traits of native Master Spirit. [30]Experience finality of liberty. [31]Natures blend creatures and Creator. [32]No possibility of casting shadow of evil. [33]One-time mortals who have attained perfection in all that pertains to will of God. [34]Only Adjuster-fused or other Father-fragment-fused beings become. [35]Threefold spirit endowment perfectly unified in. [36]Transcendation allies finaliters with spirit-gravity circuit of Eternal Son. [37]Unique beings consisting in eternal union of perfect Adjusters and perfected creatures. [38]

None become finaliters without having passed through parental relationship. [39]Not dependent on seraphic transport. [40]Recess every millennium of Havona time. [41]

Residence

5th circuit circling Salvington. [42]Ascendington is home address. [43]Have personal residences on Paradise. [44]Local system transition world #1. [45]Occupy mansion worlds in systems in light and life. [46]On Edentia; in Jerusem circles. [47]Use 1/10 of 1% of Havona worlds. [48]

Service

1 from each superuniverse portray Trinity attitude. [49]Assume duties of most celestials after morontia temple appears. [50]Attend Paradise Citizens in Havona. [51]Chief serves on Jerusem executive council. [52]May attempt trinitization. [53]Mortals serve under finaliters in light and life. [54]Nonactive in prelight-and-life domains. [55]Serve on commissions approving Havona pilgrims’ circuit advancement. [56]Serve successively in superuniverses other than those of nativity. [57]Spirit-fused mortals do not become. [58]

Tabamantia is a veteran. [59]Temple of finaliters invisible to material, early morontia vision. [60]
