Conciliating commissions

See also: UB 25:2-3.

Available no matter how trivial the misunderstanding, provided it is honest. [1]

Decisions

Are final and unanimous; no appeal. [2]Executions carried out with consent of Most High. [3]Put into effect by divine executioner. [4]Master Physical Controllers subject to conciliating commissions. [5]No decision until all evidence heard. [6]

Do not pass upon matters of eternal import. [7]Embodiment of supreme justice. [8]Fourth creatures discern spirit and matter. [9]Function upon petition of opposing parties. [10]Grow from arbiters of differences to explainers of mysteries. [11]Headquarters on superuniverse capitals as well as each mansion world. [12]Justice guides appear before. [13]Keep universe running smoothly. [14]Members are divine executioner, judge-arbiter, recorder, Spirit-advocate. [15]Members are never separated. [16]No limit to ability to call witnesses. [17]Number 100 million in Nebadon; 126 trillion in Orvonton. [18]Operate on Urantia. [19]Quartets of Universal Conciliators. [20]Travel at seraphic velocity. [21]Traveling courts for minor misunderstanding. [22]
