Dark islands of space

At astronomic center of Satania. [1]At center of Angona system. [2]Collisions of dead giants convert enormous masses into rarest energy. [3]Convert energy into matter. [4]Highly energized cold condensed spheres disintegrate matter. [5]Large aggregations of matter devoid of light and heat; function as balance wheels; locatable by gravity effect. [6]Near approach may give birth to planets. [7]Origins by accretions of transmuting energy; accumulations of cold matter; from burned-out suns. [8]Vast dynamos which Universe Power Directors use to concentrate and directionize space-energies. [9]
