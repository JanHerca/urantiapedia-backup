Saul (king)

And witch of Endor. [1]Defeat by Philistines attributed to ritual errors. [2]Joseph recounted olden history of. [3]King David married daughter of. [4]King David turned over 7 descendants of Saul to be hanged by Gibeonites. [5]Made king by his troops. [6]Rallied northern clans to withstand Ammonites. [7]Required 100 Philistine foreskins as dowry for Michal. [8]Took his life on Mount Gilboa. [9]
