Omnificence

Doctrine disenfranchises all other personalities. [1]Eternal Son not personally responsible for conduct of all spirit personalities. [2]Fallacy of omnificence is colossal error of Pantheism. [3]God does not personally do everything. [4]God has not decided all things for us in advance. [5]Jews saw God in everything that happened. [6]
