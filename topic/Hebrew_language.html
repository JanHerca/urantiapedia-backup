Hebrew language

Alphabet from Philistines; little writing until 900 B.C. [1]Distinctly Andonite. [2]Jesus spoke. [3]Not a written language at time of exodus from Egypt. [4]Spoken by priests and rabbis in times of Jesus. [5]
