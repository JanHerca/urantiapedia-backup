Seven Spirits of Havona Circuits

See also: UB 17:5.

Ascenders do not hold personal communion with. [1]Created omniaphim, with Infinite Spirit. [2]Created tertiary supernaphim. [3]Each Havona circuit ruled by one Spirit. [4]In personality registers. [5]Like Adjusters to Havoners, except that Havoners do not become one with them. [6]Local universe Mother Spirits train with. [7]Master Spirits collectively produced, in response to Supreme Being. [8]Not concerned with regimes of Eternals of Days. [9]Not part of original pattern universe. [10]Servants of Seven Master Spirits. [11]Uniform impersonal representation of Infinite Spirit and Seven Master Spirits to Havona. [12]Work confined to Havona. [13]
