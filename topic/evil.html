Evil

See also: UB 130:1.5-6; UB 132:2.

Adjusters pilot God-conscious mortals away from. [1]Almost every human being has a pet evil. [2]Always results when personal evaluations are elevated to absolutes. [3]Cano assured Eve persons with good motives could do no evil. [4]Choosing to become children of evil. [5]

Dealing with evil

3 ways of resisting evil. [6]Avoid evil by seeing things as they are. [7]Do not fight evil with its own weapons. [8]Do not to others what you would not have done to you. [9]Fear not resistance of evil. [10]Fret not because of evildoers. [11]Friendship is insurance against evil. [12]Go forward in righteousness or retrogress into evil. [13]Hate evil. [14]Love of truth necessary for victory over evil. [15]Meditation brings deliverance from evil. [16]Nonresistance to evil. [17]Overcome evil with good. [18]Permit evil to run full course of own bankruptcy. [19]Prayer mobilizes soul to withstand evil. [20]Privilege to cleanse ourselves from all evil. [21]See evil against background of ultimate good. [22]Socrates taught not to return evil for evil. [23]Spiritual rebirth essential to deliverance from evil. [24]When no opening for evil, sin cannot be entertained. [25]

Defined as

Darkness which follows rejection of light. [26]Righteous ends are evil when achieved in wrong way. [27]Gravity-resisting on mental and spiritual levels. [28]Having origin in misuse, distortion, perversion. [29]Immature choosing which, when willfully endorsed, becomes sin. [30]Imperfect obedience to Father’s will. [31]Inevitable if creature is to be free. [32]Inherently, automatically suicidal when undiluted. [33]Originating in imperfection. [34]Partial realization of, or maladjustment to, universe realities. [35]Partial creativity tending toward disintegration. [36]Produced by contrastive perfection, imperfection. [37]Remoteness from divinity. [38]Seeing sin where there is no sin, or no sin where there is sin. [39]Stimulative of choosing between truth and error. [40]Suggesting deficiency of wisdom. [41]Transgression of law. [42]

Deliver us from evil. [43]God does not create evil. [44]Human likes and dislikes do not determine evil. [45]Human nature tends toward evil. [46]Isolated and purely selfish pleasures are relative evil. [47]

Jesus

Abhorred everything which savored of evil. [48]Did not advertise evil by forbidding it. [49]Had little to say about vices. [50]Love of Jesus swallows up all evil. [51]Refused to compromise with evil. [52]Would not serve evil that worship of God might result. [53]

Most destructive of personality status is betrayal and disloyalty to confiding friends. [54]Partial knowledge is potentially evil. [55]

Result of evil

Evil of one augments tribulation of all. [56]Evil proceeding from heart defiles. [57]Evil results when lesser is chosen over greater. [58]Escaping duty, we go under control of evil. [59]Good cannot result from evil to one who does evil. [60]Pain and sorrow follow in path of evil. [61]Time lag of mercy before fruition of evil. [62]
