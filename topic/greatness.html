[4] Greatest is server of all. [5] Manifested by self-control. [6] Shown in unselfish labor for worthy beings in need and distress. [7] Spiritual greatness consists in understanding love. [8] Synonymous with divinity. [9] True magnitude of survival character. [10]

Worldly greatness

Apostles desired preference. [11] Refrain from contending for greatness. [12]
