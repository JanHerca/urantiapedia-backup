Catastrophic physical events, appalling accidents, horrific disasters, painful illnesses, and world-wide scourges, as time passes, all these difficult and more or less mysterious situations always work out for the welfare and progress of the universes. [1] Caligastia betrayal and Adamic default was a double disaster. [2] Collisions revivify old burned-out suns. [3]

The endowment of imperfect beings with freedom entails inevitable tragedy, and it is the nature of the perfect ancestral Deity to universally and affectionately share these sufferings in loving companionship. [4] If physical catastrophe should doom a planet, all survivors carried away to new world. [5] Jesus wascheerfully indifferent toward any impending disaster. [6]
