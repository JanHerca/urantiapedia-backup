Serapatatia

See also: UB 75:3-5.

Brown-tinted leader of Nodite Syrian confederation. [1]Cain fulfilled predictions of. [2]Drowned himself from fear, remorse. [3]Honest; never conscious of being used by Caligastia. [4]Impatient. [5]Nodite leader with whom Eve privately met. [6]Tribes of Serapatatia began bitter warfare with Adamites. [7]
