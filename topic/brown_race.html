Present-day brown men descend from mix of yellow and red races in Asia. They held islands off the Asiatic coast. [1] At some point in time they begun a civilization on the near-by islands of the Pacific. [2] Serapatatia was of brown race, a brown-tinted man, a brilliant descendant of the onetime chief of the Dalamatia commission on health. [3]
