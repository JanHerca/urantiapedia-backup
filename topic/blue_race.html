Adamites preferred; infiltrated by 15,000 B.C. [1] Best absorbed by Andites; unfit exterminated. [2] Biologic basis of white race. [3] Cro-Magnoids. [4] Early tamed the elephant. [5] Highly blended with yellow and red races. [6] In central and South America. [7] In India. [8] Misfortune so largely lost. [9] Mixed with Andonites. [10] Moved into Europe; drove Andonites from. [11]

Nature

Artistically gifted. [12] Brain power of red race, sentiment of yellow race. [13] Expert hunters, fishers, boatbuilders. [14] Most aggressive, adventurous Eurasians. [15] Perfectly honest, wholly free from sexual vices. [16] Tendency to fight among themselves. [17]

Neanderthal absorption retarded. [18] One ruler had 100,000 burden bearers. [19]

Orlandof

Great teacher among blue race 200,000 years ago. [20] On 24 counselors. [21]

Partial to alphabet writing. [22] Persisted longest in southern France. [23] Perverted by Caligastia. [24] Profited most from Dalamatia teachings. [25] Scattered all over Europe. [26] Somewhat broad-headed. [27] Survived in Basques and Berbers. [28] United with Adamites, who exterminated Neanderthal strains to produce Cro-Magnons. [29] Usually subdues indigo race. [30]
