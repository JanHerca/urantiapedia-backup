Adamson

Adam’s first son. [1]Company of 27 followed Adamson northward from garden. [2]First wife, 32 children left for Edentia after the default. [3]Founded civilization about 35,000 B.C. [4]Grew wheat and barley. [5]Lived 396 years. [6]Survival of traditions about Adamson in Greece. [7]

And Ratta

67 children; every 4th often invisible. [8]Grandparents of 2000 secondary midwayers. [9]Married when Adamson was 120. [10]Sato was a direct descendant of. [11]
