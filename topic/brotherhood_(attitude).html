Brotherhood (attitude)

Achieve by

Appeal to Adjuster. [1]Approaching other religions by truths in common. [2]Economic interdependence and social fraternity. [3]Ethical awakening. [4]Intellectual cross-fertilization. [5]Political wisdom. [6]Religious revelation. [7]Social fraternity. [8]Spiritual insight. [9]

Barriers to brotherhood

Antagonisms natural. [10]Doctrine betrays man into intolerance, bigotry. [11]Lack of natural brotherly attraction. [12]Prophets should not strengthen barriers to brotherhood. [13]Races seldom take kindly to. [14]Ecclesiastical authority. [15]

Cf: no peace between truth and error. [16]Delight in bearing one another’s burdens. [17]Inevitable clash between religions of spirit and authority. [18]Invariably manifests in unselfish and loving service. [19]Lack of spiritual brotherhood inexcusable and reprehensible. [20]Micah: live each according to his understanding of God. [21]Most powerful, most tenuous factor of civilization. [22]Unless religious, brotherhood is an abstraction devoid of love. [23]
