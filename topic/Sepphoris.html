Sepphoris

Important trade center; Herod Antipas rebuilt; capital of Galilee. [1]Jesus told apostles to avoid. [2]Jesus worked as smith in. [3]Joseph killed working on Herod’s palace in. [4]Low moral standards. [5]Rebecca daughter of Ezra moved to. [6]Tiberias succeeded Sepphoris as capital of Galilee. [7]
