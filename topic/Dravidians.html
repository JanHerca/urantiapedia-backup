Dravidians

See also: UB 79:3.

Absorbed Aryan conquerors, overran most of India. [1]Among earliest peoples to build cities. [2]Andite conquerors of India blended with natives. [3]Centers in river valleys. [4]Founded superior civilization 12,000 years ago. [5]
