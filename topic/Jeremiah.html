Jeremiah

Fearless prophet. [1]Asserted Yahweh was God of all nations. [2]Counseled surrendering Jerusalem to Nebuchadnezzar. [3]Sunk in mire of filthy dungeon. [4]Proclaimed downfall of Jerusalem. [5]Proclaimed era of inner righteousness. [6]Said human heart is deceitful. [7]Told Jews of their impending doom. [8]Told of law written in men’s hearts. [9]Jesus quoted. [10]
