System Sovereigns

See also: UB 45:2.

3 have rebelled in Nebadon. [1]709,841 primary Lanonandeks designated as System Sovereigns in Nebadon. [2]Cabinets of Planetary Princes advise. [3]Chiefs serve for dekamillennium. [4]

Functions

100 sit in council on constellation headquarters. [5]Appear on planets in light and life to elevate Planetary Princes to Planetary Sovereigns. [6]Dispatch and direct Planetary Princes, Material Sons, ministering spirits. [7]Dispatch Material Sons to planets. [8]Enforce mandates of constellations and local universes. [9]Ex-Sovereigns teach on Lanonandek worlds. [10]Perpetually head local systems in light and life; no more rotation. [11]Rule local systems. [12]

House of System Sovereigns functions on constellations in light and life. [13]Not supervised by Trinity observers. [14]Rarely consent to narration of planet’s affairs on another. [15]Rule in commissions of 2 or 3. [16]Selected by supreme council; appointed by Constellation Father. [17]Wide powers of personal discretion. [18]
