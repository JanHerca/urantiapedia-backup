Ambitions

Abraham’s surrender of personal ambitions. [1]Belief in charms destroyed ambitions. [2]Bowing down before idols of selfish. [3]Buddhists taught all human striving distasteful. [4]

Dangerous

Until fully socialized. [5]When motivated by self-seeking or vengeance. [6]

Enslave men to debt. [7]Few mortals dare to draw available personality credits. [8]Forceful ambitions essential to material success. [9]Futile without an audience to parade before. [10]Jesus repeatedly dashed his apostles’ ambitions. [11]Many will not persist in morontia; true ambitions satisfied. [12]May thwart maternal instinct. [13]Opposed by animal indolence. [14]Spirit-born are calm while fondest ambitions perish. [15]Virtually destroyed by Vedic doctrines. [16]Will be finished in eternity – if worth finishing. [17]
