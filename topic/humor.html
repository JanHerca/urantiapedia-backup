Humor

Angels enjoy our real humor. [1]Echoes of a backward glance; based on recalling past. [2]

Function

Automatic safety valve preventing sustained self-contemplation. [3]Demonstrates absurdity of much causing concern. [4]Discounts anxieties of present. [5]Divine antidote for exaltation of ego. [6]Lessens shock of unexpected fact or truth. [7]Prevents injurious nervous tension. [8]

Hearty when portraying one’s supposed superiors falling victim. [9]Jesus’ rare good humor. [10]Jews lacked. [11]Joys of Existence seek to improve. [12]Legacy of Adamic race, Andites. [13]Primitive men have no capacity for. [14]Religion exalts all true. [15]Reversion directors promote. [16]

Spirit humor

Grows out of faith in God’s loving overcare. [17]Higher spirits engage in reminiscent humor. [18]Never accentuates misfortunes of weak and erring. [19]Reminiscent, current, and prophetic types. [20]

Urantian humor is vulgar and unkind, but comparatively keen. [21]
