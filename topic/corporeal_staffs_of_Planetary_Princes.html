Corporeal staffs of Planetary Princes

See also: UB 50:3-4; UB 66:2-7.

Connecting link with world races. [1]Exempt from most diseases; subject to certain accidents. [2]Immortality sustained by tree of life. [3]Life Carriers provide with new physical bodies. [4]Mate among themselves; seldom with world races. [5]Perform physical work in building headquarters city. [6]Primitives look upon with awe and reverence. [7]Procreate primary midwayers, high material beings. [8]Progeny are administrators in light and life. [9]Unfused citizens of system capitals. [10]Unfused morontia progressors; Adjusters detached during service. [11]Usually removed from planet upon arrival of Adam. [12]
