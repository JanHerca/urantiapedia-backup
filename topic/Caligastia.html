Titular Planetary Prince until deposed in times of Jesus. [1] Advocated cause of Lucifer on Urantia. [2] Deposed Planetary Prince of Urantia; the devil; evil one. [3] Abaddon was chief of staff of. [4]

Before rebelling

Secondary Lanonandek #9344. [5] Attached to Life Carriers on Jerusem. [6] Elevated to Lucifer’s personal staff. [7] Repeatedly denied commission as Planetary Prince. [8] Planetary Prince of Urantia. [9] Arrived 500,000 years after appearance of will, with appearance of colored races. [10] Dalamatia commandments enjoined obedience to. [11] Corporeal staff of.

Rebellion

Patiently warned regarding pride, critical tendencies. [12] Proclaimed God of Urantia by Daligastia. [13] Ideas for premature freedom failed. [14]

Personal characteristics

Experienced, brilliant, original mind. [15] Ultraindividualistic; restless; resented supervision; tendency to disagree with established order, join parties of protest. [16] Fell in love with himself. [17] Deliberate sin completely distorted his personality. [18] Invisible to humans. [19] Misguided high Son of local universe. [20]

Power of Caligastia

Contested Moses’ passage to mansion worlds. [21] Curtailed by incarnation of Melchizedek. [22] Enemy would seek to draw apostles away. [23] Never able to oppress or coerce normal individuals against their wills. [24] No power to enter men’s minds unless they so desire. [25] Opposed Adam’s mission though shorn of most power. [26] Now shorn of all power to harm. [27] Shorn of authority during incarnation of Michael. [28] Since Pentecost, servile before Adjusters, Spirit of Truth. [29]

Sin of Caligastia

Bypassing time governor of progressive liberation. [30] Perverted planetary schools. [31] Premeditated betrayal of trust. [32] Prostituted divine talents for own aggrandizement. [33] Tried to get ahead of slow, sure way of accomplishing divine purpose. [34]

Adam and Eve arrival

Held many conferences with Adam. [35] Failed to involve Adam and Eve in rebellion. [36] Serapatatia not aware of being used by. [37]

Jesus arrival

Immanuel instructed Michael to adjudicate. [38] Fully visible to Jesus on Mount Hermon. [39] Jesus defeated. [40] Forever silenced by Jesus’ life of self-forgetful service. [41] Judas’s betrayal pleased. [42] Council seat of Caligastia occupied during time of tomb. [43] Michael offered mercy to. [44] Still free on Urantia. [45]
