Guardian angels

See also: UB 113:0-1.

After subjects’ death

Accompany defeated wards if remanded. [1]Attend youths in probationary nursery. [2]Group guardians reassemble their subjects in resurrection halls. [3]Hold survivors’ souls as sacred trust. [4]Indispensable to terrestrial escape. [5]Part with mortal associates before flight to Havona. [6]Respond to roll call of justice for nonsurvivors. [7]Sponsor subjects’ new morontia form. [8]Survivors first see guardian angels on mansion worlds. [9]

All volunteer for their assignments. [10]Arrive on inhabited worlds with first Adjuster. [11]Assigned in accordance with human intelligence, spirituality, and destiny. [12]Called guardians of destiny when assigned to a single human. [13]Came in great numbers in days of Onagar. [14]Cherubim and sanobim always associated with guardian angels on earth, but not on mansion worlds. [15]Many who rebelled have repented; now detained. [16]May serve on a planet for ages; become custodians for thousands of souls. [17]Midwayers indispensible to. [18]Number hairs of head. [19]Seraphic guardians ministering to groups. [20]Study in schools for seraphim on mansion worlds. [21]

Technique of service

Act to make best use of course mortals choose. [22]Constantly manipulate mortal environment to augment cosmic insight. [23]Do not invade sanctity of human mind or manipulate human will. [24]Help mortals independent of prayers, appeals. [25]Intrude only in emergencies, on direct orders. [26]Most active when Adjusters are least active. [27]No communication with subjects’ indwelling Adjusters. [28]Physical contacts with mankind are very unusual. [29]
