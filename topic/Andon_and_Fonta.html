Andon and Fonta

See also: UB 62.5; UB 136:4.5.

Actual parents of all mankind. [1]Lived at beginning of 3rd ice age, a difficult, invigorating environment. [2]Born in India. [3]

Family into which born

Father killed by hostile gibbons. [4]Great trial to Primates parents. [5]Never popular with Primates cousins. [6]Parents learned to throw stones and use clubs. [7]

Their great decission

Nearly lost lives on numerous occasions. [8]Noontide tryst, decision to flee. [9]Named by archangel. [10]Named each other Sonta-an and Sonta-en. [11]

Their descendants

19 children. [12]Eskimos are sole unmixed survivors of. [13]

Achievements

Andon discovered fire. [14]Andon taught dispute settlement by beating tree. [15]Confused philosophy; almost fire worshippers. [16]Worked out improved sign and word language. [17]Possessed personality. [18]Indwelt by Adjusters. [19]

Death and present

Killed at age 42 by falling rock. [20]Loss would not have prevented human evolution. [21]Fused with Adjusters on Jerusem. [22]Survival of. [23]Never returned to Urantia; sought to send greetings. [24]Welcome pilgrims on 1st mansion world. [25]
