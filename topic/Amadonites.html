Amadonites

55 translated to Jerusem; remainder stayed. [1]144 loyal Andonites including Amadon. [2]Descendants of Andonites who remained loyal with Van and Amadon; more cultural than racial term. [3]Joined Nodites to become Vanites. [4]Traditional enmity with Nodites. [5]
