Edentia

See also: UB 43:1.

After Satania rebellion, archrebels continued to attend conclaves on. [1]Construction. [2]

Description

10 Supreme Power Centers, 10 mechanical controllers, and 10 frandalanks on. [3]70 major satellites are 10 times size of Urantia; 700 sub-satellites are size of Urantia. [4]Animal life of Edentia is intelligent and serviceable; not carnivorous. [5]Edentia is 100 times larger than Urantia. [6]Half devoted to gardens of God. [7]Magnificent highlands. [8]No rugged mountains, oceans, torrential rivers, storms, seasons. [9]Sea of glass. [10]Spheres energized by space currents. [11]Thousands of lakes and interconnecting streams. [12]Tree of life is shrub of. [13]Usual three-gas atmosphere. [14]Working model of Edentia on Jerusem. [15]

Ethical sensitizers in brotherhood schools. [16]Headquarters of Norlatiadek, our constellation. [17]Legislative assemblies. [18]Melchizedek colleges. [19]
