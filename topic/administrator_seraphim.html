The acting ruler of Urantia is assisted by a thousand of them. [1]

They form the fourth order of the Seraphim and are assigned to the administrative tasks of the local systems. They are native to systems capitals but are also found in architectural spheres and inhabited worlds. [2]

Many of this order were lost in the Lucifer rebellion. It was the most damaged order. Manotia was a loyal angel who managed to save two-thirds of them, but a third were lost. On Urantia the situation was more disastrous as about half of the Seraphim administrators supported the cause of Lucifer. [3] Their residence is in the Jerusem circles, specifically in the fourth circle, and they constitute a very large group. [4]

Types

They are organized for service in this way:

  1. Administrative assistants.
  2. Justice guides.
  3. Interpreters of cosmic citizenship.
  4. Quickeners of morality.
  5. Transporters. [5]
  6. Recorders. [6]
  7. Reserves. [7]
