On planets such as ours the highest form of life is reproduced by a life-carrying bundle which possesses twenty-four pattern units. On Urantia there are forty-eight units of pattern control—trait determiners—in the sex cells of human reproduction. [1] The staff of the Planetary Prince had chromosomes reorganized by the life circuits of the Satania system. [2]
