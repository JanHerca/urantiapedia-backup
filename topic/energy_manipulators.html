Energy manipulators are the fifth group of celestial artisans. They are divided in following types: [1]

  1. Physical-energy manipulators.
  2. Mind-energy manipulators.
  3. Spiritual-energy manipulators.
  4. Compound manipulators.
  5. Transport advisers.
  6. Experts of communication.
  7. Teachers of rest.
