Consciousness

See also: UB 16:9.

Cannot be explained by electronic association. [1]Embraces awareness of personality actuality; ability to recognize other personalities; freewill. [2]Freedom to choose only exists within realm of one’s. [3]Inherent quests for knowledge, morality, spirit, and personality values. [4]Mind endows consciousness in absence of personality. [5]Most primitive consciousness is accumulation of protoplasmic memory material. [6]Not inherent in matter. [7]Proceeds from fact to meaning to value. [8]Resides in adjutant mind-spirits. [9]Rests gently upon electrochemical mechanism below and spirit-morontia system above. [10]

Self-consciousness

Can jeopardize reception of some spiritual truth. [11]Dependent on innate other-awareness. [12]Is not the soul. [13]

Supreme is living way to transcendence of consciousness. [14]Trust Adjusters for all matters beyond dead level of consciousness. [15]
