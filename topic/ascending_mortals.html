The path of ascension

Six stages of spiritual existence are provided for ascending mortals; and the existence of a seventh stage is postulated. [1] The seven stages of the ascending universe career are: [2]

  1. Planetary mortals.
  2. Sleeping survivors.
  3. Mansion world students.
  4. Morontia progressors.
  5. Superuniverse wards.
  6. Havona pilgrims.
  7. Paradise arrivals.

During ascension stages ascending mortals must pass through different abodes:

  • Progression of survivors from Urantia. [3]
  • Residence in Jerusem circles of the ascending mortals. [4]
  • The residential area on Edentia, located in the mid-zone of the thirty-fifth triangle. [5]
  • The minor sector of his local universe. [6]
  • All the ten superuniverse major sectors. [7]
  • Swing around superuniverses many times. [8]
  • Ascendington. [9]

There are seven jubilees celebrations during the ascension career: [10]

  1. The first jubilee, the agreement with the Thought Adjuster to survive.
  2. The second jubilee, the awakening in the morontia life.
  3. The third jubilee, the fusion with the Thought Adjuster.
  4. The fourth jubilee, the awakening in Havona.
  5. The fifth jubilee, the finding of the Universal Father.
  6. The sixth jubilee, the Paradise awakening from the final transit slumber of time.
  7. The seventh jubilee, entrance into the mortal finaliter corps and the beginning of the eternity service.

The process of ascension

In the local universe

The mortal mind initiates its career as a material mechanism and is eventually mustered into the Corps of the Finality as a well-nigh perfected spirit existence, becoming progressively less subject to material gravity and more responsive to spirit gravity. [11]

As we ascend inward in the scale of life, will we retain the ability to recognize and fraternize with the fellow beings of our previous and lower levels of experience. [12] At each new translation or resurrection we add one more group of spiritual beings to our vision range, which is made possible by the Adjusters. [13] As ascendant mortals we are in possession of personal memories of all former and lower existences. [14] On the Salvington spheres we will be evolving from a morontia being to the status of a true spirit; we will be more spiritual than material. [15] The mother influence dominates the human personality throughout the local universe childhood of the growing soul. [16] The new being resulted from Adjuster fusion is of the eternal past as well as for the eternal future. [17]

Before departing from their native local universes for the superuniverse receiving worlds, the mortals of time are recipients of spirit confirmation from the Creator Son and the local universe Mother Spirit. [18]

Although ascending mortals were vulnerable to Lucifer rebellion, they withstood the sophistries of rebellion better than the lower spirits; not a single member of the Satania ascendant citizenship resident on Jerusem participated in the Lucifer rebellion. [19]

In the superuniverse

As the ascending mortal passes beyond the borders of his local universe he is not deprived of the Spirit of Truth. [20]

The distinctive personality trends exhibited in the life experience of ascending mortals, which are characteristic in each superuniverse, and which are directly expressive of the nature of the dominating Master Spirit. [21] Ascending mortals serve in constellation legislatures. [22] Who fail in Deity adventure remands to superuniverses waiting to resume the Paradise ascent. [23] When ascendants are fused with the Adjuster partakes the threefold spirit endowment of the evolutionary realms. [24]

In Havona

Havona provide the stimulus of all human impulses towards the attainment of true spirit values on the highest conceivable reality levels. Havona is the pre-Paradise training goal of every ascending mortal. [25] The Seventh Spirit, the fostering adviser of all triune-origin beings, is also the adviser and director of all the ascending pilgrims of Havona. [26] Mortal associates, the companion in our earthly careers, often we remain together for all the ascension towards Havona. [27] Ascendants study about Supremacy in Havona. [28] Ascendants are trained to administer universes. [29] They are trained to the limits of their capacity respecting every detail of every divine principle of the just and efficient. [30]

In Paradise

In Paradise we must fraternize with upwards of three thousand orders of Paradise Citizens. [31]

Names

We are reckoned as ascending sons of God the instant fusion with Thought Adjuster takes place. [32] Mortals are denominated ascending sons after fusion, before that they are denominated faith sons. [33] Grandfanda was first ascending mortal to attain Havona. [34]

Destiny

Ascending mortals can become:

  • Celestial artisans. [35]
  • Celestial Overseers. [36]
  • Evangels of destiny. [37]
  • Finaliters. [38]
  • Mighty Messengers. [39]
  • Technical Advisers, as temporary members. [40]
  • Those High in Authority. [41]
  • Those without Name or Number. [42]

Ascendant Adjuster-fused mortals compose the bulk of the primary Corps of the Finality. [43] Ascending mortals become Adjuster-fused Mortal Citizens of Paradise. [44] Ascending mortals are recordwd in personality registers. [45] Since Adjusters are fragments of an absolute God, ascending mortals may one day attain God as absolute. [46] For those who entertain the Mystery Monitors, there is indeed no limit to the possible heights of their spiritual ascent and universe attainment. [47] It is not known the “finality destiny” of the ascendant mortals of time. [48]

Jesus as ascending mortal

Michael's fifth bestowal was to incarnate as Eventod, an ascending pilgrim of mortal origin on Uversa, for eleven Orvonton-years. [49]

In one short life, did Jesus traverse that experience of religious spiritual progression which man begins on earth and ordinarily achieves only at the conclusion of his long sojourn in the spirit training schools of the successive levels of the pre-Paradise career. [50]
