Twins

Alpheus twins q.v. Andon and Fonta q.v. Mid-mammal ancestors. [1]No two exactly alike. [2]Primate ancestors. [3]Believed caused by magic or infidelity; one usually killed. [4]Regarded as very lucky or very unlucky. [5]Simian ancestors. [6]
