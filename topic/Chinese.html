Chinese

Live peaceably with fellows. [1]Achieved high civilization apart from Andites. [2]Andite cultural and spiritual legacy. [3]Apt students of warfare; relentless, aggressive. [4]Children require little discipline. [5]Difference between northern and southern races. [6]Early river settlements disrupted by floods. [7]First to establish settled communities. [8]Improved tone of bell by human sacrifice. [9]Jesus’ talk with Chang. [10]Cumbersome ideographic writing. [11]Learned to plant seeds. [12]Most advanced social development on Urantia. [13]Now reawakening. [14]Numbered 12 million in 15,000 B.C.; aggressive militarists; spoke common language. [15]Pacific tendencies. [16]Raised animals by 5000 B.C. [17]

Religious beliefs

Basis of ancestor worship. [18]Degenerated into swarms of devils and dragons. [19]Dragon worship is survival of snake cult. [20]Employed magic to protect against demons. [21]First to achieve freedom from abject fear of gods. [22]Regarded disease as result of evil demons. [23]Soul and body remained together after death. [24]

Singlangton assumed leadership of. [25]Sons of Han. [26]Statehood delayed by thoroughness of conquest. [27]Treated women better than most. [28]Unexcelled in family loyalty, group ethics, personal morality. [29]Yin and yang teaching. [30]
