Paradise Companions

See also: UB 25:8.

Assigned to ascenders in order of waiting. [1]In Uversa personality register. [2]Nominated by local universe Mother Spirits, Reflective Spirits, and Majeston. [3]None assigned if mortal companion or seraphic guardian present. [4]Often assisted by Paradise Citizens. [5]Recruited from seraphim, seconaphim, supernaphim, and omniaphim. [6]Same one remains with pilgrim until associates arrive or individual is mustered into Corps of Finality. [7]

Service

Follow defeated pilgrims until they return to Havona. [8]Greet pilgrims on arrival on Paradise. [9]Is highest honor for ministering spirit. [10]Is not permanent. [11]Nothing especial to accomplish; simply provide companionship. [12]

Study ascenders’ backgrounds before arrival. [13]
