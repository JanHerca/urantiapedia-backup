Flesh

See also: UB 34:7.

All things earthly are evanescent. [1]Be not discouraged by discovery you are human. [2]Concept of mortification of flesh attached to Christianity. [3]

Flesh and spirit

All pass through conflict traversing from flesh to spirit. [4]Avoid wasteful, weakening conflicts of the flesh. [5]Flesh does not naturally bear fruits of Spirit. [6]Mortals from normal worlds do not experience constant warfare with spirit. [7]Refereeing conflicting contentions of flesh and spirit. [8]Spirit is easy victor over flesh. [9]That which is born of flesh is flesh. [10]

Freedom from desires of mortal life in Buddhism. [11]Futility of pinning all hope on temporal goals. [12]High purposes of great mind antagonized by urges of primitive inheritance. [13]Inherent nature derived from animal-origin races. [14]Is of little profit. [15]Jesus’ faith destroyed every conflicting desire. [16]Put not trust in uncertainties of. [17]Urges.

Urges

Fickleness of sex urge. [18]Greatest passions are lust, anger, pride, deceit, greed. [19]Inherited urges cannot be altogether modified. [20]Normal; not in conflict with highest spiritual attainment. [21]Not suppressed by religious rebuke or laws. [22]Of selfishness and animal nature. [23]Recognize temptation for exactly what it is. [24]Sensual gratifications alone do not confer happiness. [25]Usually caused by one’s own debased tendencies and natural propensities rather than the devil. [26]

Jesus’ m. is to do will of God. [27]
