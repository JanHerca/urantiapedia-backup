Dance

Among primitives

Many shamanesses were professional dancers. [1]Originated in seasonal worship festivals. [2]Purely military dance refined by Prince’s staff. [3]Successor to military drill. [4]Was dramatic prayer. [5]

Crude and grotesque attempt to approach celestial harmony of being placement. [6]Wild dance can induce mystic state. [7]
