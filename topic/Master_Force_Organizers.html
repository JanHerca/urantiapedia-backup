Types of Master Force Organizers.

# Primary Eventuated

See also: UB 29:0.2.

Bring energy to level of initial response to Paradise gravity. [12]Inaugurated energy whirl of Andronover then withdrew at right angels. [13]Modify absoluta into segregata by their passive presence. [14]Nebulae creators. [15]Transmute segregata into puissant energy. [16]

# Associate Transcendental

See also: UB 29:0.3.

Comprise Council of Equilibrium. [17]Leave local universe when Creator Son arrives. [18]Make force-charge of space gravity responsive. [19]Metamorphose cosmic force in outer space. [20]Retire upon appearance of linear gravity response in favor of Universe Power Directors. [21]Take over from Primary Eventuated Master Force Organizers when energy begins to respond to Paradise gravity. [22]Transmute puissant energy into ultimatons and back to puissant energy.
