The Adjuster is our compensatory equalization of the enormous universe tension which is created by the distance of man’s removal from God. [1]

There are many compensations for rebellions as Lucifer's: one is enhanced careers of those mortal ascenders, the Jerusem citizens, who, by withstanding the sophistries of sin, placed themselves in line for becoming future Mighty Messengers. [2]

Although the Universal Father is personally present in the residential universe, he does not indwell the minds of the beings originating in that universe. [3] There are, of course, certain compensations for tribulation, such as Michael’s bestowal on Urantia. [4] Gains of higher loyalty offset earlier tribulation. [5] Gods take note of man’s needs at every stage. [6]

It will undoubtedly be the function of the evolutionary citizens of the grand universe to compensate the outer-spacers for this deprivation of the growth of Supremacy. [7] Nevertheless, there is a certain, inevitable penalty attaching to mortal existence on such advanced evolutionary planets. [8]

In the time-evolving universes of space there is greater divinity attenuation, more difficult problems to be solved, and larger opportunity to acquire experience in their solution. [9] Nebadon’s troubles made it an educational clinic for other near-by creations. [10]

Isolation of the spheres affords their races a unique opportunity for the exercise of faith and for the development of a peculiar quality of confidence in cosmic reliability. [11]

Ascending mortals, in their long and loving association with the Havona natives, do much to compensate for the spiritual impoverishment of the earlier stages of mortal progression. [12]

Son and Spirit fusion do, superficially, resemble compensations of unexplained failures in some detail of the Paradise-attainment plan, but is not true. All these happenings unfold in obedience to the established laws. [13] Spirit-fused mortals attain unsurpassed wisdom regarding local universe. [14]

The evolving Supreme will eventually compensate finite creatures for their inability ever to achieve more than limited experience contact with the universe of universes. [15] Those personalities who are farthest from God, and who can therefore experience the greatest degree of spiritual ascent in the universe. [16]

Universe unfailingly applies equalizing techniques designed to insure that no ascender shall be deprived of aught which is essential to his ascension experience. [17]
