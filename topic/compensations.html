Compensations

Adjusters equalize tension of man’s removal from God. [1]Enhanced careers of ascenders in rebellion. [2]Father personally present in Paradise and Havona, but does not indwell minds of their residents. [3]For tribulation. [4]Gains of higher loyalty offset earlier tribulation. [5]Gods take note of man’s needs at every stage. [6]Grand universers will compensate outer-spacers for deprivation of growth of Supremacy. [7]Inevitable penalty for life on advanced planets. [8]More difficult problems allow larger opportunity to acquire experience in their solution. [9]Nebadon’s troubles made it an educational clinic. [10]Quarantined spheres afford opportunity for faith. [11]Relationship between Havona natives and ascenders. [12]Son- and Spirit-fusion in ascendant plan. [13]Spirit-fused mortals attain unsurpassed wisdom regarding local universe. [14]Supreme will compensate for inability to achieve unlimited. Experience. [15]Those starting farthest from God have greatest ascent. [16]Universe unfailingly applies equalizing techniques. [17]
