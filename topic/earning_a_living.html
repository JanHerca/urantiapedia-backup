Earning a living

Apostles organized to provide everything needful. [1]Be content with wages. [2]Economic necessities tie man up with reality. [3]Father provides food for every one who seeks. [4]Jesus’ financial struggle. [5]Manifold difficulties of earning a living. [6]Personal earnings may be used as one sees fit. [7]Prosperity requires one function in some channel of material wealth. [8]Providing for oneself is sacred trust. [9]Supinely trusting in a fictitious Providence. [10]Those who give lives to gospel shall live by gospel. [11]Use moral power and spiritual energy to solve problems. [12]Wages fell to 25¢/day in Nazareth. [13]Work persistently to better one’s life. [14]Workers’ salaries in continental nation. [15]
