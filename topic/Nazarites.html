Nazarites

Abner onetime head of. [1]Ascetic and eccentric; majority refused to accept Jesus. [2]Believed in coming of Messiah. [3]Engedi was southern headquarters. [4]John the Baptist joined. [5]Looked upon as sanctified personalities. [6]Nazarite corner of women’s court in Jerusalem temple. [7]Parable of Lazarus and Dives. [8]Permitted to enter holy of holies in temple. [9]Samson and prophet Samuel were. [10]Vowed to abstain from intoxicants, let hair grow, not touch dead. [11]
