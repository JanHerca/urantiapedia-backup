Immanuel

See also: UB 18:6; UB 33:5.

Union of Days number 611,121 assigned to Nebadon. [1]Michael’s elder brother. [2]So named because representative of Father. [3]Supervises Faithfuls of Days. [4]

Lucifer's rebellion

Knew of Lucifer’s disaffection for 500 years. [5]Counseled Michael to allow rebellion to self-obliterate. [6]

Regarding Michael’s bestowal

Bestowal commission to Michael. [7]Vouched for Michael’s safety prior to arrival of Adjuster. [8]Assumed authority during Michael’s bestowals. [9]Messages to Jesus. [10]After baptism, Jesus recalled instructions of. [11]Jesus’ bestowal release. [12]Suggested Jesus continue bestowal to end. [13]

Michael sovereignty

Has never acknowledged subordination to Michael. [14]Participates in executive affairs only upon request. [15]Would have left Nebadon had Michael asserted premature sovereignty. [16]Michael requested Immanuel forever to remain in Nebadon. [17]
