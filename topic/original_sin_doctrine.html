Original sin doctrine

Accounted for death; weakened grip of magic. [1]Belief Adam’s sin cursed human race. [2]Crass injustice of damning soul because of ancestors’ sin. [3]Doctrine of hereditary guilt, innate evil, and redemption. [4]Doctrine of total depravity destroyed religion’s potential. [5]Erroneous teaching. [6]Fall of man. [7]Paul’s theory partly Mithraic, partly original. [8]Required hypothesis of onetime golden age. [9]Soul looked upon as being born under forfeit. [10]Superstition that man is child of devil. [11]
