Kenites

Canaanite tribe that revered Salem religion. [1]Moses’ father-in-law was of; Moses influenced by. [2]Non-Hebrew nomadic Palestinians; joined David’s new kingdom of Judah. [3]Progeny of Abraham through Isaac intermarried with. [4]
