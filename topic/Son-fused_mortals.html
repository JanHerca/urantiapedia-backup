Son-fused mortals

See also: UB 40:8.

Adjusters serve during life in flesh up to local universe capital; build up souls. [1]Ancients of Days approve Son-fusion. [2]As ascending sons. [3]Assist Celestial Guardians. [4]Fusion implemented in Reflective Spirits. [5]Fusion is union with individualized bestowals of Creator Son. [6]In personality registers. [7]May become Trinitized Ambassadors, Trinitized Sons of Attainment, or Trinitized Sons of Selection. [8]Provide each superuniverse with permanent ascendant citizenship. [9]Range Orvonton; sometimes journey to Paradise; number less than one million. [10]Some ascend to Paradise. [11]Supervise abandonters. [12]Synchronizing difficulty inhibited Adjuster fusion. [13]Urantia mortals will not become. [14]Why some are Son-fused. [15]
