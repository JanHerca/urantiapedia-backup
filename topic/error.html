Ability to entertain error lost on final spirit levels. [1] Adam and Eve’s default was error, not rebellion. [2] Causes sorrow because it cannot be realized. [3] Conflicting truth and falsehood create. [4]

Dealing with error

Deal with gently. [5] Jesus did not attack e., rather embellished truth. [6] Living truths drive out serious error. [7] Overthrow by love of living truth. [8] Sometimes too great to rectify by revelation. [9]

Defined as

Inevitable result of immature creatures’ freewill contact. With reality. [10] Inherent in evolutionary growth. [11] Misconception or distortion of reality. [12] Possibility of unwise judgment. [13] Shadow of relative incompleteness. [14]

Difficult for angels and midwayers to conceive of brilliant, trusted rulers like Caligastia going astray. [15] Greatest error is craving power to deprive others of liberty. [16] May markedly delay evolution of soul. [17] Memories of past life without spiritual meaning will perish with material brain. [18] Suggestive of lack of intellectual keenness. [19]
