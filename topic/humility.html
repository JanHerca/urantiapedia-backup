Humility

Before God is appropriate. [1]Causes one to be exalted. [2]Childish when self-conscious and attention-craving. [3]God dwells with the contrite and humble. [4]Happy are the humble, the poor in spirit. [5]Humbly receive credit for Adjuster’s endeavors. [6]Jesus asked why someone called him good. [7]Lean not upon one’s own understanding. [8]Normal consciousness of smallness, mortal shortcomings, essential for religious growth. [9]Once was attempt to deceive envious spirits. [10]Parable of Pharisee and publican. [11]Purge me from secret sins. [12]Safety only when soul craves reproof. [13]Sit not in chief seat. [14]Spiritual progress predicated on recognizing spiritual poverty. [15]
