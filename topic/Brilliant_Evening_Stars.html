Brilliant Evening Stars

Also known as superangels. [1]Easily visible to morontians. [2]

Functions and actions

Accompany Magisterial Sons on bestowals. [3]Advised Melchizedek receivers after rebellion. [4]Assist Trinity Teacher Sons in light and life. [5]Bid bestowal Sons to be about Father’s or brother’s business. [6]Close working connection with finaliters. [7]Destiny guardians report to after subjects’ deaths. [8]Direct seraphic armies. [9]Form examining corps on Jerusem. [10]In charge of 6th Salvington circuit. [11]Liaison with Gravity Messengers. [12]One attended upon Jesus throughout his bestowal. [13]One is liaison of 24 councilors with Michael. [14]One or more represent Gabriel on each constellation and system. [15]Order was planned by Melchizedeks. [16]Portray teachings of finaliters in light and life. [17]Represent Gabriel at Salvington conclaves. [18]Represent Trinity Teacher Sons on planetary missions. [19]Sponsored Papers 37, 47, 85–89, 119. [20]Teach on Jerusem. [21]

In personality registers. [22]Liaison officers of Gabriel. [23]

Nature

In group Universe Aids. [24]None went astray in Lucifer rebellion. [25]Possess spirit force manifestable independently of personal presence. [26]Superangels created by Creator Sons and Mother Spirits. [27]

Number 13,641 in Nebadon; 4832 created, 8809 attained. [28]Residence in Jerusem circles. [29]Seraphim may become. [30]System headquarters on 4th transition world. [31]Usually work in pairs, one created and one ascendant. [32]
