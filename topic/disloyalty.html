There are degrees of disloyalty: the partial loyalty of indecision; the divided loyalty of confliction; the dying loyalty of indifference; and the death of loyalty exhibited in devotion to godless ideals. [1] Of all forms of evil, none are more destructive of personality status than betrayal of trust and disloyalty to one’s confiding friends. [2]
