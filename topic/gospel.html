Acceptance of gospel immediately improves home life. [1] Believers must love gospel more than mother, father, wife, husband, children, or siblings. [2] Believers undependable unless gospel experienced in heart. [3] Belongs to all who believe, not merely to priests. [4] Common people hear gladly. [5] Difficult to perpetuate prior to printing. [6] Do not try to fit into old forms of religious belief. [7] Dominated by truth conviction. [8] Evidence of belief in gospel is love shown to one another. [9]

Explained

Applies to individual, not state. [10] By faith every mortal may have salvation. [11] By faith becoming spirit-conscious of sonship with God. [12]

Cardinal features

Sacredness of the individual. [13] Satisfactions of loving service. [14] Spiritual fellowship with Father. [15] Spiritual is transcendent over the material. [16] Will as determining factor. [17]

Divine sonship, spiritual liberty, eternal salvation. [18] Fasting not part of. [19] Fatherhood of God, sonship-brotherhood of men. [20] Founded on fact of Jesus’ God-revealing life. [21] Friendship with God. [22] Gift of eternal life received by faith. [23] God is Father, we are his sons; believing this is salvation. [24] Jesus’ teachings, not his personality. [25] Joy and liberty of new kingdom. [26] Love of Father and service of his children. [27] Not dependent on death or resurrection of Jesus. [28] Revelation of goodness of God. [29] Salvation by faith alone; liberty through truth. [30] Seek first God’s kingdom and his righteousness. [31] Sermon on Mount is not the gospel. [32] Solvent for spiritual difficulties. [33] Truth, beauty, goodness, love, and worship meaningful as fellowship with man, sonship with God. [34] Unfolds truths of being. [35]

Frees men from burdens of formal religion. [36] He who knowingly denies gospel shall be denied by angels. [37] Hidden from wise and self-righteous. [38] Infectious in its transforming power. [39] Jesus dwells in all who believe his gospel. [40] Left as Jesus presented, all religions would have accepted. [41] Loyalty to gospel dependent on sound early home training. [42] Melchizedek receivers proclaimed gospel to Adam. [43] Messengers. Must show increasing vitality, depth of spiritual power. [44]

Proclaiming the gospel

Accord generous recognition. [45] All Urantia waiting for. [46] Allow apostolic teachers to teach own views. [47] Always respect personality of man. [48] Approach others recognizing common truths. [49]

Avoid

Appeal to fear, mind, religious hysteria, emotion, pity, or sentiment. [50] Artificial situations. [51] Attacking old ways, reforming mores; rather add new truth. [52] Becoming embroiled in politics. [53] Dishonesty, unfairness, ignoble tactics. [54] Distracting details, flowery language. [55] Diverting devotion from one duty to proclaim gospel. [56] False sympathy; self-pity; mere fellowship in misery; withhold pity from cowards and quitters. [57] Identifying with a particular race, culture, political system. [58] Indulging in sarcasm or cynicism. [59] Overpowering arguments, appeals, or miracles. [60] Promoting righteous cause by force. [61] Requiring God-conscious men to reject their historic leadership. [62] Striving. [63] Subordination of gospel to physical ministry. [64] Substituting wonders and healing for. [65] Use of law or force to compel. [66]

Be aggressive, unafraid, even to ends of earth. [67] Be bold and emphatic. [68] Be more gracious, less stringent in collateral social requirements. [69] By asking or answering questions. [70] Cannot reveal God to those not seeking. [71] Challenge uncertainty of unbelievers by profound faith and. Certainty. [72] Clash only when rulers throw themselves in way of gospel. [73] Deferred rewards inherent in service for truth. [74] Duty of those who know spirit realities. [75] Effort attended by varying degrees of success. [76] Employ analogies, true stories to illustrate one central truth, not fables or allegories. [77] Extent necessity to go to the people reveals lack of righteous life. [78] Fear not those who can kill the body. [79] First get man securely within kingdom. [80] Freely you have received, freely give. [81] Go into all the world, to every man, woman, and child. [82] Instruction for teachers and believers. [83] Live so as to lead others to inquire. [84] Make provision for own support. [85] Morality is disseminated by moral fragrance. [86] No longer be concerned with commands to refrain. [87] No requirement to subscribe to things repugnant, unholy, or untruthful. [88] Only law to obey is command to preach gospel. [89] Persistent preaching will result in unbelievable liberation. [90] Personal experience with God is only positive proof. [91] Preach to all without fear or favor. [92] Proclaim kingdom not just with words but in life. [93] Proclaim whole truth, not just part. [94] Promulgate gospel only by power of spirit. [95] Recipients felt they were conferring with Jesus, not confessing to him. [96] Reiterate message. [97] Restate form of expression as language is revised. [98] Second-milers needed. [99] Show diligence in gaining souls. [100] Spirit of Truth gives strong urge to proclaim. [101] Spiritual experiences will not convince unbelievers. [102] Strange preacher not to be forbidden. [103] Suit presentation to audience. [104] Those who give lives to proclaiming gospel shall live by gospel. [105] Uncertain troublous future in proclaiming gospel. [106] Whet appetites of associates for truth. [107]

Will triumph and be proclaimed to all nations. [108]
