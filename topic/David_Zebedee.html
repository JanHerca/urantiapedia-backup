David Zebedee

See also: UB 156:6.3-4; UB 157:6.1; UB 158:8.1; UB 176:2.9; UB 177:5.1,3; UB 182:2.5; UB 187:6.1; UB 188:3.3.

The dependable David Zebedee. [1]Respected Jesus as mechanic, but took little stock in his religious views. [2]Father Zebedee went to live with. [3]Followed Jesus after draught of fishes. [4]

Work as follower

Fearless and efficient leader. [5]Served of his own heart. [6]Supervised Bethsaida encampment. [7]Plunged Kirmeth into lake. [8]Set up big camp at Pella. [9]Closed camp at Pella, sold equipment. [10]Gave Judas funds from equipment sale. [11]

Guarded Jesus

Jesus declined offer of guards by. [12]Ordered messengers to safeguard Jesus. [13]Posted 25 sentinels about Zebedee house. [14]Stood guard at Gethsemane. [15]

Death of Jesus

Knew of plan to kill Jesus; Judas’s part. [16]Sent for Salome and Jesus’ family. [17]Believed Jesus would rise from tomb. [18]

After Jesus death

Dropped Judas’s bag in Matthew’s lap. [19]At Jesus’ 4th appearance. [20]Married Ruth, left for Philadelphia after crucifixion. [21]Financial overseer of kingdom at Philadelphia. [22]Self-appointed defender of Lazarus. [23]
