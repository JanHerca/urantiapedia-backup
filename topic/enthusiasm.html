Enthusiasm

Be possessed with zeal for kingdom. [1]Jesus surcharged with divine enthusiasm for the gospel. [2]Loveless zeal always harmful to religion. [3]May become fanaticism. [4]Peter’s enthusiasm commendable but dangerous. [5]Work at what one finds to do with all one’s might. [6]Zeal shown in unrecognized religious activity. [7]
