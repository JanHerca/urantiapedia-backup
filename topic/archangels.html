Archangels

See also: UB 37:3.

100 assigned to Magisterial Sons on planetary missions. [1]Absolve destiny guardians if subjects fail to survive. [2]Activities on Jerusem. [3]Angels of the resurrection. [4]Commission of Life Carrier transmutation. [5]Created by Creator Son and Mother Spirit. [6]Dedicated to creature survival and ascension career. [7]Destroyed mortal body of resurrected Jesus. [8]Divisional headquarters on Urantia. [9]Do not normally serve under Gabriel. [10]In charge of 7th Salvington circuit. [11]In group Universe Aids. [12]In personality registers. [13]Message naming Andon and Fonta. [14]Message to Life Carriers recognizing life on Urantia. [15]Nebadon’s archangels directed by first-born. [16]None went astray in Lucifer rebellion. [17]Number 800,000 in Nebadon. [18]Preserve records of personality for resurrection. [19]Resurrect primitives for Spirit fusion. [20]Speak in confirmation of fusion. [21]Supervise translations in morontia temple. [22]Throne of presiding archangel on Jerusem surrounded by 24 counselors. [23]Wrote Papers 33, 35, 41, 44, 46, 48, 77-81. [24]
