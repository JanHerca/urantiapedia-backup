Body, human

[4]Energized by circulatory distribution of assimilable energy products of nourishment. [5]Gland chemistry effects imagination, spirit reception. [6]Healing secretions when injured. [7]Human cells akin to disease-producing organisms. [8]Living electrochemical mechanism of animal origin. [9]Mortals may be outwardly beautiful, inwardly unlovely.
