Sincerity

Ascenders’ sincerity is sublime by reaching Havona. [1]Even in material-mindedness, Philip was sincere. [2]Eve’s project of modifying the divine plan was sincere. [3]Key of the kingdom. [4]Most serviceable when wedded to discretion. [5]Of prayer is assurance of its being heard. [6]Shadow of hair’s turning, premeditated for untrue purpose, is falsehood. [7]Sometimes causes pain. [8]
