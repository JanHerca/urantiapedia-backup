Pride

Arrogance may evolve into sin. [1]Commendable in family, reputation, and nation if not carried too far. [2]Contemplating God, self-glorification is ridiculous. [3]Deceitful, intoxicating, and sin-breeding. [4]Exaltation of one’s own mind over God’s. [5]Father disdains. [6]Futile without audience to parade before. [7]Goes before destruction. [8]Greatest danger to man’s spiritual integrity. [9]Lucifer’s and Caligastia’s exaggerated feelings of self-importance. [10]Lucifer’s pride nourished itself to self-deception. [11]Material irritant of immaturity. [12]Obscures God. [13]Outruns reason and eludes logic. [14]Pride of unspiritualized learning is treacherous. [15]Prevented by intimate contact with others. [16]Resist not injury to feelings of personal dignity. [17]Subtleties of spiritual pride. [18]Wisdom of recognizing one’s insignificance. [19]
