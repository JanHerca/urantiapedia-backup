Dogs

At first only howled; later barked. [1]Brought Abel’s flocks home without their master. [2]Cannot know God. [3]Charged swine at Kheresa. [4]Detected approach of ghosts. [5]Eat crumbs which fall from children’s table. [6]First adopted by blue man. [7]First animal to be domesticated; used for food, hunting, transportation, companionship. [8]Held in high esteem as pet and as food. [9]In Miocene, Oligocene. [10]In North American ice age. [11]Jesus and Ganid played with dogs. [12]Present not what is holy to. [13]Sacred animal of Parsees. [14]Watchdogs made it possible to sleep at night. [15]
