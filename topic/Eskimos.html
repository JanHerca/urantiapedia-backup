Eskimos

Believe man comprised of body, soul, and name. [1]Believe soul stays with body 3 days after death. [2]Chance meeting with red tribe. [3]Conceive everything in nature has a spirit. [4]Fairly free from violent antagonisms. [5]Meager concepts of God. [6]Mothers lick babies in lieu of washing. [7]Naturally docile children thrive on little discipline. [8]

Origin

Blend of Andonite and blue races. [9]Descendants of Foxhall peoples. [10]Sole survivors of Urantia aborigines; Andonites resembled. [11]

Penalty for crime decreed and administered by family wronged. [12]Seldom cannibalistic except in famine. [13]
