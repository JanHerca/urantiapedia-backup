Samuel

See also: UB 97:1.

First Hebrew prophet. [1]Nazarite. [2]Priests recorded Saul crowned by. [3]Proclaimed Yahweh all-powerful, changeless Creator. [4]Rough-and-ready reformer; overthrew Baal sites; chopped captive king in pieces. [5]Spoke of anger of the Lord. [6]Sprang from long line of Salem teachers. [7]
