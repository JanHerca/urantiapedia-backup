There have been many events of religious revelation but only five of epochal significance. These were as follows:

  1. The first was Dalamatia teachings, performed by the 100 corporeal members of Prince Caligastia’s staff. [1]
  2. The second was Edenic teachings, by Adam and Eve and their descendants. [2]
  3. The third was the one from Melchizedek of Salem. [3]
  4. The fourth was the one from Jesus of Nazareth. [4]
  5. The fifth is Urantia Papers. [5]

Will it be the sixth in 1000 years? Not known, although some hints in the Urantia revelations seem to suggest it. [6]
