Creativity

Can be haphazard or directed and constructive. [1]Can be turned to destructivity. [2]Diminished by matings between inferior strata. [3]Divine creativity unfailingly characterized by unity. [4]Inner creativity contributes to ennoblement of character. [5]Reflective of antecedent creator-spirit activities. [6]Reflective Spirits were created upon Master Spirits’ attainment of perfect synchrony with Trinity. [7]Will aspiring to be Godlike. [8]
