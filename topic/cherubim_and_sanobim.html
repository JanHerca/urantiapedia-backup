Cherubim and sanobim

See also: UB 38:7-8.

500 million pairs on Urantia in 1934. [1]Celestial artisans teach efficient energy intake to. [2]Chief advised Melchizedek receivers after rebellion. [3]Created by local universe Mother Spirit. [4]Directed by seraphim. [5]Engage in self-improvement; learn languages. [6]In personality registers. [7]Like midwayers; near morontia level of existence. [8]Lowest order of angels; almost human. [9]Many lost in Lucifer rebellion. [10]May attain full seraphic standing. [11]Mother Spirit transforms deserted cherubim and sanobim into Mansion World Teachers. [12]Remain in local universe. [13]

Service

Associated with seraphim; keep records. [14]Deliver undeveloped souls to probationary nurseries, where they have educational facilities. [15]Help Spirit-fused survivors repossess mortal memory. [16]Not with guardian angels on mansion worlds. [17]Perform indispensable borderline tasks. [18]Routine spirit workers. [19]Very limited in solitary function. [20]

Some rebel cherubim and sanobim on earth up to Christ’s death. [21]

Types

Cherubim is right-hand deflector, positively charged angel; controls pair. [22]Fourth creatures. [23]Positive or aggressive and negative or retiring. [24]Sanobim is left-hand deflector, negatively charged angel, complement of being. [25]
