Jealousy

Can turn love into hate. [1]God jealous for man, not of man. [2]Mental poison; interferes with spiritual progress. [3]Among primitives, older spouses delighted by new wives. [4]Primates jealous of Andon’s tree house. [5]Sexual jealousy not innate. [6]When attacked by j., rest in citadel of spirit. [7]
