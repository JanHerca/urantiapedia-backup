Evangelists

A group of preachers ordinated by Jesus from the original evangelists. Trained by learning and doing. [1]Training at Bethsaida. [2]75 of 117 survived test; 1/3 deserted. [3]Selection committee. [4]Abner in charge. [5]Did not have everything in common like apostles. [6]Jesus told to work in disregard of what might happen. [7]Went forth wholly unprovided for. [8]Numbered 70 only by coincidence. [9]Ordination. [10]Return to Pella. [11]Unseen messengers by sides of. [12]
