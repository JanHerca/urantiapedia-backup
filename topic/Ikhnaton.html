Ikhnaton

See also: UB 95:5.

Pharaoh of Egypt; one of most remarkable persons in history. [1]Clear concept of Salem religion. [2]Established monotheism under guise of sun god Aton. [3]Swung whole nation from polytheism to monotheism. [4]Discredited priests. [5]Went too fast; failed to provide stability, prosperity. [6]

Influence

Influenced El Shaddai concept. [7]Mesopotamian religion entered Hebrew literature through. [8]1st Psalm is heart of teachings. [9]Repercussions of I.’s work persisted in Palestine and Greece. [10]Descendants gave Jesus Greek translation of Scriptures. [11]
