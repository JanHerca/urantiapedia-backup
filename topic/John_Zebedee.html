John Zebedee

See also: UB 129:1.12; UB 139:4; UB 141:7.14; UB 143:3.5; UB 143:5.12; UB 144:1.7; UB 178:2.6-8; UB 192:1.3; UB 192:4.7.

Actions

Baptized by John in December A.D. 25. [1]Became apostle. [2]Claimed preferred seat at Last Supper. [3]Collected Palm Sunday donkey. [4]Fishing partner of Andrew, Peter, and brother James. [5]Forbade strange preacher. [6]Helped bear Jesus’ body to tomb. [7]Laid hands on converts receiving Spirit of Truth. [8]Led Jesus to pool of Bethesda. [9]Married brother James’s widow. [10]Personal agent regarding Jesus’ family. [11]Purchased Capernaum house for Jesus. [12]Searched 40 days for Jesus. [13]Selected Judas Alpheus. [14]

Assigned as personal aide to Jesus. [15]

Attitude

Apostle of new commandment. [16]Became dominated by brotherly devotion. [17]Believed Jesus had risen. [18]Believed Jesus would return before his death. [19]Bid fire devour insolent Samaritans. [20]Conceited, bigoted and intolerant; self-contained. [21]Cool and daringly courageous; faithful, devoted. [22]Favored proclaiming Jesus king. [23]First apostle to believe in resurrection. [24]Grasped significance of entry into Jerusalem. [25]Impetuous. [26]Naturally thought in symbols. [27]Never overcame disinclination to talk. [28]Son of thunder. [29]

Book of Revelations written by

See also: UB 139:4.14.

Four and twenty elders reference. [30]

First Epistle written as cover letter for Gospel of John. [31]

Gospel of John

Avoided all reference to casting out devils. [32]Has viewpoint of Alexandrian Christians, disciples of Philo. [33]Inspired by John Zebedee; written by Nathan. [34]Jesus’ avoidance of writing delayed. [35]Presents triumphant Jesus in full consciousness of divinity. [36]

Jesus’ admonitions to. [37]Jesus took to Jerusalem. [38]Outstanding theologian. [39]

Present

Among 120 at Pentecost. [40]As designated observer at Jewish court. [41]Asleep in Gethsemane. [42]At crucifixion. [43]At empty tomb. [44]At Jesus’ appearances. [45]At Jesus’ examination by Pilate. [46]At transfiguration. [47]In adjoining room during Jesus’ humiliation. [48]Near Jesus after his arrest. [49]

Public preacher. [50]Questions of. [51]Referred to as a prophet. [52]Right-hand support of Peter. [53]Salome asked preference for. [54]Special instructions to. [55]Used term “eternal life” for “kingdom of heaven”. [56]

Visions of

Lucifer rebellion. [57]Mortals on Jerusem. [58]New Jerusalem in post-Teacher Son era. [59]Salvington and Jerusem. [60]

Well known to Annas’s older servants. [61]Youngest apostle, unmarried, from Capernaum; 24 in A.D. 26. [62]
