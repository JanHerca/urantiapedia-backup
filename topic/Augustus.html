Coemperor with Tiberias Caesar; died in A.D. 14. [1] Decreed census in 8 B.C. [2] Founded Nicopolis. [3] Temple in Caesarea. [4] Tried to destroy mysteries, revive older political religion. [5] Worship of Augustus became Roman religion. [6]
