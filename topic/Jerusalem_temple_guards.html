Jerusalem temple guards

Type of soldiers from the temple. In conference with Judas. [1]Dispute with Roman soldiers over where to take Jesus. [2]Before tomb of Jesus. [3]Fled when stone rolled away from Jesus’ tomb. [4]Bribed to say disciples took Jesus’ body. [5]Mocked, spit upon, buffeted Jesus. [6]Threw Jesus’ burial linen over nearby cliff. [7]
