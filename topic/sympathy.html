Sympathy

Avoid leaning upon false sympathy. [1]Cannot develop strong characters out of self-pity. [2]Crave no undeserved sympathy. [3]Do not appeal to pity in proclaiming gospel. [4]Jesus seldom indulged in pity. [5]Offer not sympathy to those who lie down before troubles. [6]Overmuch sympathy may degenerate into emotional instability. [7]Sooner uprooted in rebels’ hearts by allowing free expression of evil. [8]Suspicion incompatible with. [9]Without sentimentality. [10]
