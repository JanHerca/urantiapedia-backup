Fruits of the spirit

Believers show; religious experience unfailingly yields. [1]Convincing evidence of spiritual certainty consists in. [2]

Defined as

Enjoyable and ennobling self-control. [3]Life reactions of spirit-led mortal. [4]Simply traits which Jesus manifested. [5]Substance of the Supreme. [6]Yielding to fellows what one has received. [7]

Enumerated

Intellectual peace, social progress, moral satisfaction, spiritual joy, and cosmic wisdom. [8]Love, joy, peace, long-suffering, gentleness, goodness, faith, meekness, temperance. [9]Service, devotion, loyalty, fairness, honesty, hope, trust, ministry, goodness, tolerance, peace. [10]

Father requires we bear. [11]God’s will discernible as fruits of spirit in the spirit-led. [12]Must be brought forth to continue ascension. [13]
