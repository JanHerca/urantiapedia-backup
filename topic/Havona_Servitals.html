Havona Servitals

See also: UB 25:1.

1/4 who achieve divine embrace never return. [1]7 Universal Conciliators created for each Servital. [2]138 billion serve on Uversa. [3]Assist, and eventually become, Graduate Guides. [4]Creatures of Seven Master Spirits and Seven Supreme Power Directors. [5]Fourth creatures discern reality of spirit and matter; are semiphysical. [6]Help ascenders on superuniverse capitals. [7]In personality registers. [8]Involved in phases of Supremacy. [9]“midway creatures” of Havona. [10]Number is prodigious; more being created. [11]Resemble individual Master Spirits. [12]Stay with same pilgrim throughout his ascent. [13]
