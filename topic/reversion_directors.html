Reversion directors

See also: UB 48:4.

[5]In Uversa personality register. [6]Lessen irritability and resentment in groups. [7]Maintain headquarters on each mansion world. [8]Operate chiefly from local system headquarters. [9]Promote relaxation and humor, reverting memory to former state, thought change, mind rest. [10]Recruited from Havona natives to morontia progressors, who predominate. [11]Theater on Jerusem. [12]
