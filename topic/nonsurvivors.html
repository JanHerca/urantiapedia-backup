Nonsurvivors

Adjusters conserve realities born of love and service. [1]Are moving counter to stream of universe events. [2]Become as if they had never been. [3]Delay Supreme in achieving divinity expression. [4]Failure to survive is never due to neglect by Adjuster. [5]Final divergence of personality from trend of universe. [6]Guardian angels respond to dispensational roll call, but Adjusters make no answer. [7]Pass into realm of the unrealized. [8]Personalities of nonsurvivors absorbed into Supreme. [9]Possibility of cosmic self-destruction unavoidable if personality is to be free. [10]Wholehearted identification with evil is nonexistence. [11]Withdrawing from God, man approaches nonreality – cessation of existence. [12]
