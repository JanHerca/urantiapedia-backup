Disciples

Jesus requires life of his disciples. [1]Jesus subjected disciples to repeated disappointment. [2]May return home to minister to loved ones. [3]Must be willing to forsake father, mother, wife, and child for kingdom. [4]Must earn own bread, contribute to those who teach. [5]Must renounce all they are, dedicate all they have. [6]Only 3 men refused Jesus’ invitation to become. [7]Disciples versus apostles. [8]We are Jesus’ disciples if we do Father’s will, love one another. [9]
