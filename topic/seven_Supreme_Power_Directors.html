Seven Supreme Power Directors

See also: UB 29:1; UB 116:5.4.

Act on phenomena below level of gravity energy. [1]Created by Seven Master Spirits; each in eternal subservience to one. [2]Each administers power for 1 superuniverse; administer Havona collectively. [3]Existed from near eternity. [4]In personality registers. [5]Modify, manipulate, and directionize energy. [6]Not subject to administrative direction by superuniverse governments. [7]Part of God the Sevenfold. [8]Physical energy regulators of grand universe. [9]Power potential of Almighty Supreme centered in. [10]Recruit Solitary Messengers for exploration. [11]Resist gravity. [12]Semimaterial; quasi-physical. [13]Stationed on peripheral Paradise. [14]With Seven Master Spirits, created all Universe Power Directors and Havona Servitals. [15]
