Divinity

[3]Comprehended in worship, service, and personal spiritual experience. [4]Consciousness of divinity is progressive spiritual experience. [5]Divine technique in approach to divinity. [6]Enriched by creature and Creator experience. [7]Evil inherent in segmentalized conception of divinity. [8]Fruits are peace, progress, satisfaction, joy, wisdom.
