Martha (sister of Lazarus)

See also: UB 127:3.5; UB 141:9.2; UB 164:3.1; UB 186:0.1.

At Jesus’ 4th appearance. [1]At resurrection of Lazarus. [2]Disposed of possessions, joined Lazarus at Philadelphia. [3]Distracted by numerous needless tasks. [4]First met Jesus. [5]Gave expression to no doubt. [6]Jesus loved Martha with fervent affection. [7]Martha loved Jesus like a brother. [8]One year older than Jesus. [9]Wealthy; inherited extensive vineyards and olive orchards. [10]
