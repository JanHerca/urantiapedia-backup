Virtue

Cardinal virtues of Socrates were wisdom, courage, temperance, and justice. [1]Carried to extreme, virtue may become vice. [2]Is righteousness; conformity with the cosmos. [3]Is volitional. [4]Morality is virtue. [5]Morality is virtue. [6]Not truly acquired until acts make one worthy of it. [7]Proportion required in exercise of. [8]Realized by consistent choosing of good over evil. [9]Supreme virtue is wholeheartedly to do Father’s will. [10]
