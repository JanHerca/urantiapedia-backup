Among primitives

Death believed to be penalty of sin. [1] Feared d., which liberated another ghost. [2] House destroyed in which death occurred. [3] Inexplicable. [4] Shamanic theories; witchcraft. [5]

Physical death

Adjuster departs for Divinington after destruction of mind circuits. [6] Advanced mortals exempt from. [7] Body remains on planet. [8] Guardian angels after their subjects’. [9] Helpful to view with cheerfulness. [10] In physical battle is easy. [11] No time consciousness in. [12] No death on Paradise. [13] Occurs upon cessation of measurable brain energies, vital motion. [14] Pilot light in human mind disappears upon. [15] Reflective phenomenon may occurs just prior to. [16]

Significance of death

Brings new dispensation of service. [17] Gains man nothing except experience of survival. [18] Only the beginning of endless career. [19] Part of mortal life. [20] Precipitates events which lead to Jesus. [21] Technique of escape from material life. [22]

Spiritual death

Adjuster, seraphim must concur in, Censors verify. [23] Adjuster proceeds to bosom of Father upon. [24] Adjuster released after rejection of survival; seraphim stays. [25] Doom final only after self-concepts fully displace spirit nucleus. [26] Guardian angel responds to dispensational roll call, but Adjuster makes no answer. [27] Jesus warned of spiritual doom. [28] Not sealed until power lost to choose Father’s will. [29]
