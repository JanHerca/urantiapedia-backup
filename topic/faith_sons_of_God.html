Faith sons of God

Certain reply to unbelievers. [1]

Characteristics

Believe in God of love who fosters survival. [2]Borrow best from others rather than denounce worst. [3]Certain of salvation. [4]Co-operate from unity of ideals and purposes. [5]Common people hear gladly. [6]Conscious of universe citizenship, contact with superhuman power. [7]Dedicated to wholehearted service of brotherhood of man. [8]Delivered from isolation. [9]Demonstrate fruits of spirit; risk all to find God. [10]Detached from purely temporal, trivial matters. [11]Difficulties stimulate. [12]Dispense wealth in spirit of stewardship. [13]Disregard selves, take up responsibilities daily. [14]Emancipated from haste, painful stress. [15]Enjoy comparative deliverance from the flesh. [16]Establish kingdom in hearts of mankind. [17]Ever find it easier to do right. [18]Ever more skillful in alluring fellows. [19]Exist only to love one another as Jesus loved us. [20]Fulfill threefold duty to man, God, and fellowship. [21]Go right on worshiping God in spite of anything. [22]Have implicit faith in triumph of righteousness. [23]Have same eternal life which raised Jesus from the dead. [24]Indwelt by Spirit of Truth. [25]Jealously guard life purpose from temptation to transient attainment. [26]Jesus lives anew in each. [27]Knowing God and desiring to do his will, are divinely stable. [28]Led by Spirit of God. [29]Live and triumph irrespective of crushing overload. [30]Live as if in presence of the Eternal. [31]Live as to permit Father to reveal himself. [32]Live up to responsibilities and privileges. [33]One with Jesus. [34]Only individuals who can love others as themselves. [35]Promote peace and harmony among various groups of sincere believers. [36]Renew spirits by worshipful communion. [37]Show companionship with Jesus in their lives. [38]Spirit of Truth draws together, leads into all truth. [39]Spirit will teach how to answer judges. [40]Trust and confide in one another. [41]Trust God as Jesus trusted God. [42]Trust in God’s goodness despite bitter disappointment and crushing defeat. [43]Unconcerned if all things earthly crash. [44]When Father wills, desire of faith sons IS. [45]

Having complied with group’s membership requirements, should enjoy liberty of expression. [46]Identify self with universe, serve universe family. [47]Jesus prayed for spiritual unity among. [48]Mortals are denominated faith sons prior to fusion. [49]Only reward is joy and communion in this life, eternal life hereafter. [50]Second-milers needed to win souls. [51]Spirit bears witness with our spirits that we are. [52]

Warnings

Children of world wiser than. [53]Dependable only when gospel experienced in hearts. [54]Faint-hearted believers will turn away. [55]Grave danger in times of nominal acceptance of gospel. [56]Instruction for. [57]Languish in futile yearning from not obeying truth. [58]Must function in society as individuals, not as groups. [59]Must not be troubled by deceivers, wars, famines, earthquakes. [60]Must not resort to physical force for kingdom. [61]Must not try to mold others to some pattern. [62]Necessity to take gospel to people shows failure to live righteous life. [63]Only battle is against doubt. [64]Relationship with Jesus must take precedence over all others. [65]Too vacillating and indefinite in teaching conduct. [66]Who bear not fruits of spirit are dead. [67]Will be hated by all men for Jesus’ sake. [68]Will be killed by those who think they do God service. [69]

Where 2 or 3 are gathered together. [70]
