Atmosphere

See also: UB 58:2; UB 59:6.12.

Appeared as result of heated interior and cooling crust. [1]Blanketing effect. [2]Carbon dioxide in early atmosphere afforded luxuriant growth. [3]Ends with auroral streamers 400 miles up. [4]Enriched with oxygen. [5]Ideal for animal respiration 160 million years ago. [6]Of morontia spheres is oxygen, nitrogen, morontia gas. [7]Present on all evolutionary planets if not too small. [8]Shades into average space 3000 miles out. [9]Slowly evolving 1-1/2 billion years ago. [10]
