Complaining

Accept challenges without. [1]After Michael’s incarnation, no complaining by Material Sons. [2]Elders complained about Jesus to Joseph and Mary. [3]Judas complained about Nathaniel. [4]Laborers in vineyard complained they weren’t paid more. [5]Man should not bemoan his lowly origin when Creator Sons. Pass through same experience. [6]Man should not complain of experiences part of life. [7]Over petty harassments and fictitious grievances. [8]Prayer turns complaining into patience. [9]To suffer evil without complaining is insufficient. [10]
