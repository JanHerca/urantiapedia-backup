Eugenics

See also: UB 82:6.

Adamic age usually witnesses elimination of unfit. [1]Adamites, Andites exterminated inferiors. [2]All primitive tribes killed deformed and sickly children. [3]Civilization in danger when youth ignore. [4]Curse of deteriorated, antisocial, feeble-minded, and outcast specimens. [5]Early war destroyed unfit peoples; modern war the opposite. [6]False sentiment fosters and perpetuates hopelessly defective human strains. [7]Greek culture perished due to imported slaves. [8]In continental nation. [9]In hunting era, tribes destroyed those deemed inferior. [10]In progressive civilization. [11]Matings between inferior strata diminish creativity. [12]Nonsurvival by disinheritance due to inferior ancestors. [13]Poverty cannot be eliminated if defectives reproduce without restraint. [14]Prejudice against crossbreeding. [15]

Recommended societal actions

All-important work of eliminating defectives and degenerates. [16]Biologic disfellowshiping of more markedly unfit, defective, degenerate, and antisocial stocks. [17]Biologic fitness through selective reproduction in light and life. [18]Difficulty in executing eugenics in absence of competent judges. [19]Folly of allowing superiors to mate with inferiors. [20]Foster normal man; keep subnormal under control. [21]Racial interbreeding disastrous under present conditions. [22]Scientific intelligence must supersede uncontrolled natural selection. [23]Unfit should be eliminated before racial blending. [24]

Sex slavery polluted biologic fitness of superior peoples. [25]
