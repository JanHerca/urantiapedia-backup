Ends versus means

Everything nonspiritual in human experience, excepting personality, is a means. [1]Insight enables discrimination between ends and means. [2]Jesus never resorted to ignoble tactics. [3]Jesus would not serve evil that good might presumably derive therefrom. [4]True relationships are ends in themselves. [5]Work is less important than way it is done. [6]
