Guardians of destiny

See also: UB 113; UB 39:8.5-8.

After subjects’ death

Custodians of mind patterns and soul realities between death and resurrection. [1]Forever in communication with former wards. [2]Hold survivors’ souls as sacred trust. [3]Possible immediate resurrection for mortals with. [4]Retain soul, static formulas of identity, essential to repersonalization. [5]Sponsor subjects’ new morontia form. [6]Tribunals must absolve from blame if subjects fail to survive. [7]

Assignment

2 assigned to each mortal; complement of being is recorder. [8]According to circle attainment, spiritual consecration, enrollment in reserve corps. [9]Brought Adjusters prior to Spirit of Truth. [10]In accordance with human intelligence, spirituality, and destiny. [11]To all 3rd psychic circlers. [12]To all surviving Adjuster-fusion mortals. [13]To reservists. [14]

Destiny

Most enter Corps of Mortal Finaliters. [15]Pair achieves spiritual bi-unification on Seraphington; fuse with non-Adjuster Father fragment. [16]Some become mind planners if wards fail to survive. [17]Surest seraphic passport to Paradise is service as guardian of destiny. [18]

Develop abiding affection for their human associates. [19]Jesus sent his personal guardian away while on Mount Hermon; had no personal guardian after his baptism. [20]Majority detach from subjects during Havona. [21]Midwayers are interpreters for guardians of destiny in light and life. [22]Rantowoc first to receive. [23]

Technique of service

Correlate, unify, and personalize impersonal spirit influences for subject. [24]Gain experience on non-Adjuster fusion worlds. [25]Influence mortals in every manner consistent with dignity of personality. [26]Midwayers indispensible. [27]Ministry more effective in light and life. [28]Statement in extenuation of difficulties. [29]
