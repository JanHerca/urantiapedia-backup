Angamon was the leader of the church of the Stoics in Rome. Jesus had a talk with him that we are given a summary of in The Urantia Book. He was a great friend of Paul of Tarsus and an ardent follower of the Christian church in Rome. [1] Angamon introduced Jesus to a wealthy man from Rome who was greatly influenced by his teachings.
