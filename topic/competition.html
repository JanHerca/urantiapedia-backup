Angels make effective use of competition to inspire service. [1] Based on narrow self-interest ultimately destroys what it seeks to maintain. [2] Co-operation more efficient in advanced civilizations. [3] Effectiveness of machines determines contending groups’ survival. [4] Essential to progress; unregulated competition breeds violence. [5] Ever pulls man down. [6] Helpfully stimulated by racial diversification. [7] On continental nation. [8] Restrictions must not abrogate individual liberties. [9] Time and circumstance select the fitter for survival. [10] Wholesome rivalry to enter schools of Planetary Princes. [11]
