Andon and Fonta, our first parents, succumbed to their fears for a time. [1] Mortal man may draw near God and may repeatedly forsake the divine will so long as the power of choice remains. [2] Jesus said: “ No man, having put his hand to the plough, if he turns back, is worthy to become a messenger of the kingdom”. [3]
