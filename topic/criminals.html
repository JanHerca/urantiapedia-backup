Jesus’ words for a condemned criminal in Corinth were: “Your fellows have adjudged you guilty; they have determined that you shall die, but there is a Judge to whom you may appeal for forgiveness, and who will judge you by your real motives and better intentions. You need not fear to meet the judgment of God if your repentance is genuine and your faith sincere”. [1]
