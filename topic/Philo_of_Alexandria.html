Philo of Alexandria

Accomplishments

A founder of Christianity. [1]Great religious teacher. [2]Harmonizer of Greek philosophy and Hebrew theology. [3]Mitigated Roman objections to Judaism. [4]Profoundly influenced religious thought. [5]

Possessed copy of Book of Wisdom. [6]Tolerated belief in reincarnation. [7]Wealthy Jew; restored concept of Trinity; glimpsed Thought Adjusters; taught atonement doctrine. [8]
