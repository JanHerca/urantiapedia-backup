He and his wife Eve were the Material Sons of Jerusem that started the adamic race in Urantia.

On Jerusem

See also: UB 74:1.

Directors of experimental energy for living forms. [1] Expert anatomists. [2] Left behind 100 children. [3]

Warned

Senior Material Sons #14,311. [4] Under oaths of allegiance to Most Highs, Michael. [5] Exhaustively instructed regarding dangers of Urantia. Mission. [6] Subject to Melchizedek receivers. [7] Not to combine good and evil. [8] Not to depart from divine plan. [9]

Arrival

Arrival authorized by Most Highs, Tabamantia. [10] Rematerialized on Urantia 37,848 years ago; 10-day process. [11] Requested Melchizedek receivers to remain; they refused. [12] Spoke Andonic dialect. [13] Administration. [14] Advised not to initiate racial uplift until family numbered 500,000; other instructions. [15] Taught agriculture. [16] Refused worship of Garden dwellers. [17]

Children

Adam and Eve bore 105 pure-line children. [18] After default, all under 20 removed to Edentia, plus 2/3 over 20 who chose to leave. [19] Education. [20] On Edentia, deprived of procreative powers. [21] Some day serve on Urantia?. [22]

Default

See also: UB 75.

Realization of default. [23] Adam defaulted, but did not become involved in Lucifer. Rebellion. [24] Adam had premeditated sex with Laotta after. [25] Adam wandered in solitude for 30 days. [26] After 117 years. [27] Caligastia contributed to. [28] Caligastia held many conferences with Adam. [29] Degradation after default. [30] Deprived races of physical nature more consonant with spiritual aspirations. [31] Did not impede biologic evolution. [32] Gabriel’s appearance to pronounce judgment. [33] Left Garden of Eden to Nodites unopposed. [34] Not permitted to remove tree of life after. [35] Personal message from Michael after. [36] Received Adjusters after default. [37] Returned Melchizedek receivers refused to advise. [38] Tragedy for Urantia. [39] Transgression was error of judgment, not sin. [40] Turning aside from the ordained way. [41]

After default

Encouraged Cain to leave second garden. [42] Family grounds covered 5 square miles. [43] Family of 1647 pure-line descendants before default, 42 after default. [44] Impatient; well-meaning but deluded. [45] Plan was to build up strong reserve of violet race before beginning upstepping. [46] Plan was to upstep red race with 1 million pure-line descendants. [47] Sent and received thought oscillations 50 miles. [48] Sometime return to Urantia?. [49] Special senses not acutely present in their children. [50] Survival of. [51] Impregnated 1682 mostly Nodite women, who produced 1570 superior children. [52] Intrusted worship and education to Nodites. [53] Lived 530 years; died of old age. [54] Michael is the 2nd. [55] No liking for war. [56] Not in ancestry of Joseph father of Jesus. [57] One of 24 counselors. [58]

Inheritance of Adam and Eve

Bequeathed more humor than music or art. [59] Creates immunity to disease. [60] Enormously upstepped peoples of Urantia. [61] Improved brain power. [62] Stimulated adventure and exploration. [63] Ancient Hebrews looked for return of. [64]

Physical nature of Adam and Eve

8 feet tall; bodies shimmered. [65] Able to see midwayers, angels, Melchizedeks, Caligastia. [66] Imbibed light and energy from space. [67] Circulatory system from physical nature and tree of life. [68] Never ate meat in regular diet; no cooking in household. [69] Did not take milk from animals. [70]

Teachings about Adam and Eve

Belief their sin cursed man. Jews sought to trace origin to. [71] Legends confused with facts of Prince’s staff. [72] Paul’s doctrine of Christ as 2nd Adam. [73] Prince’s staff held out promise of sometime arrival. [74]

Urantia problem for Adam and Eve

Found no government worthy of name. [75] Isolated, distressed, disheartened by sorry plight of world. [76] Mission fraught with danger. [77] No Adam ever on a more difficult world. [78] Problem of establishing law and order. [79]
