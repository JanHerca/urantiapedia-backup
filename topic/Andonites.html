Andonites

See also: UB 63:4.

Better Primate strains assimilated by. [1]Hebrew language was distinctly Andonite. [2]Hittites stemmed directly from. [3]Incorporated in blue and white races. [4]

Migrations

Aridity drove Andonites into Turkestan, Europe in 3000 B.C. [5]Diluted Andites in central Asia. [6]Dispersed after 20 generations. [7]Driven from Europe by blue race, from Asia by yellow race. [8]Entered Europe from Siberia; absorbed by Andites. [9]Hindrances to migration. [10]In Danube valley. [11]In India. [12]In mountainous central and southeastern Europe. [13]Into China with Andites. [14]Lived in extreme northern regions. [15]On Pacific islands. [16]Scattered over Arctic and central Asia in 15,000 B.C. [17]Settlements after Adam. [18]To Iceland, Greenland. [19]Westernmost Andonites nearly driven into sea. [20]

Named. Named. Onagar was truth teacher to Andonites. [21]

Physical characteristics

See also: UB 63:4.1.

Danubians were Andonites. [22]Had 36 teeth. [23]Lapps and Eskimos nearest to aboriginal Andonic type. [24]Not so sensitive to pain. [25]Pale skins, broad-heads. [26]Physically mature at 12; life span of 75. [27]

Practices

Affection for comrades. [28]All except Badonans practiced human sacrifice. [29]Cliff and grotto dwellers. [30]Constant battles with inferior tribes. [31]Constructed dome-shaped stone huts. [32]Eastbound groups mingled with animals. [33]Evolved animal worship. [34]Exceedingly imitative; no sense of humor. [35]Fairly free from violent antagonisms. [36]Fashioned flint tools. [37]Feared elements. [38]Fearless hunters. [39]First marine adventurers. [40]Idea of using stick and flint, bound with sinews. [41]Incessant clan warfare. [42]Lived almost exclusively on flesh. [43]Loyal to families. [44]Mixed with blue races. [45]Occupied with hunting for food, fighting to avenge. [46]Originally not cannibalistic. [47]Regarded twins as good luck. [48]Some mated with simians. [49]Taught golden rule. [50]Well-developed language. [51]Would not live with inferior tree-dwellers. [52]

Retrogressed for 500,000 years after Andon. [53]Unusual that Andonites evolved before colored races. [54]
