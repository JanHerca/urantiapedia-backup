Dreams

Absurdities of dreams testify to pressure of unexpressed emotions. [1]Celestials seldom use dreams to communicate. [2]Disordered, disconnected parade of uncoordinated sleeping mind. [3]Grotesque dreams indicate Adjusters’ failure to make efficient contact. [4]

Interpretation of dreams

Danger to regard as divine communications. [5]Largely superstitious and groundless. [6]

Ordinary dreams are purely physiologic and psychologic. [7]

Particular dreams

Claudia Procula’s of Jesus. [8]Convinced Zacharias regarding John. [9]Delivered Peter from fear of uncleanness. [10]Informed strange religious teacher of Jesus’ birth. [11]Peter’s of Jesus on Sea of Galilee. [12]Reconciled Joseph regarding Jesus’ birth. [13]Young Jesus’ sleep disturbed by revolting. [14]Zoroaster’s caused him to remodel his religion. [15]
