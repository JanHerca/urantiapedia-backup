Abraham (Old Testament)

See also: UB 93:5; UB 126:1.2.

Abraham’s original name. [1]3 celestial beings appeared to. [2]Ancestor of Joseph father of Jesus. [3]Born at Ur; left because of sun worship. [4]

In Egipt

Found distant relative on Egyptian throne. [5]Shared in spoils of Egyptian military campaigns. [6]Political circumstances facilitated A.’s promotion of Salem teachings in Egypt. [7]

In Canaan

Kingly ambitions; planned to subdue all Canaan under Salem rule. [8]Moved on Lot’s enemy with army of 4000. [9]Sacrifice of Isaac. [10]Shrewd, wealthy business man; not overly pious. [11]Slow to give up household gods. [12]Spiritual leader of all surrounding tribes. [13]Victory over Chedorlaomer. [14]

His wifes

Feared someone would kill him to get Sarah. [15]Keturah and Hagar were concubines of. [16]Married his half-sister. [17]

The covenant with Melquisedek

Believed halfheartedly in Melchizedek covenant. [18]Justified by faith. [19]Melchizedek charged Abraham to keep alive truth of one God. [20]Melchizedek revealed Most High observer to. [21]Melchizedek’s brilliant pupil, chief supporter. [22]Melchizedek’s covenant with. [23]Introduced tithing system. [24]Fearful and timid after disappearance of Melchizedek. [25]

Jews

Offspring formed nucleus of later Jewish people. [26]Abrogated Michael’s covenant with Abraham. [27]Believed national history began with Abraham. [28]Traced Abraham’s genealogy to Adam. [29]Viewed Abraham as having ordained oral law. [30]

Named by Jesus
