Caligastia one hundred

100 ex-mortals chosen by Caligastia of 785,000 Jerusem citizen. Volunteers. [1]50 males and 50 females; served as couples; couples lived together. [2]Brought to Urantia by seraphic transport. [3]Habitations provided with abundance of land. [4]Parents of 50,000 primary midwayers; each couple produced 1000. [5]40 of 100 refused to join rebellion. [6]

10 autonomous councils presided over by Daligastia

Andonite counterparts

56 loyal against rebellion; 44 rebelled. [17]55 loyalists. [18]Had complement of system circuits introduced into bodies; used tree of life. [19]Tree of life indefinitely extended mortal existence. [20]

Physical aspects

Andonic life plasm transplanted by surgeons from Avalon. [21]Before default, did not sexually reproduce. [22]Constituted sex creatures to be able to mate with Adam’s offspring. [23]Divided equally as to sex. [24]Had Andonic skin color and language. [25]Material bodies constructed for. [26]Nonflesh diet. [27]On Jerusem, had no reproductive prerogatives. [28]Tree of life indefinitely extended lives. [29]

Loyalists

All except Van transferred to Jerusem. [30]Had possession of tree of life. [31]Helped prevent obliteration of spiritual truth. [32]

Rebels

Degraded to mortal status; died natural deaths. [33]Deprived of system life circuits. [34]Driven from Dalamatia by semisavages. [35]Fate unknown; Adjusters still tarry on Jerusem. [36]Increased numbers by sexual reproduction. [37]

Work of the Caligastia one hundred

See also: UB 66:5-7.

Plan was to procreate successors prior to retiring upon arrival of Adam and Eve. [38]Adopted many Andonic and Sangik children. [39]All were agriculturists and horticulturists. [40]All worked at physical labor. [41]First introduced pottery. [42]Gathered together superior individuals. [43]No methods employed not natural on Urantia. [44]Markedly accelerated normal evolution. [45]Taught agriculture. [46]Taught Andonites, red, blue, and yellow races. [47]Used natural means of race improvement. [48]Blue man profited most from teachings of. [49]
