1 And it came to pass after this that his name during his lifetime was raised aloft to that Son of Man and to the Lord of Spirits from amongst those who dwell on the earth. [1]

2 And he was raised aloft on the chariots of the spirit and his name vanished among them.

3 Et à partir de ce jour, je ne fus plus compté parmi eux : et il me plaça entre les deux vents, entre le nord et l'ouest, où les anges prirent les cordes pour me mesurer la place des élus et des justes.

4 Et là je vis les premiers pères et les justes qui habitaient ce lieu dès le commencement.
