Author: **William S. Sadler**

Immanuel, the Union of Days, No. 611,121 of the Supreme Trinity Personalities, is the personal representative of the Universal Father and the ambassador of the Paradise Trinity to the universe of Nebadon. [UB 33:5.1](/en/The_Urantia_Book/33#p5_1)

## I. GABRIEL

1. Gabriel is the personalization of the first concept of identity and ideal of personality conceived by the Creator Son and the Creative Spirit. [UB 33:4.1](/en/The_Urantia_Book/33#p4_1)
2. Gabriel is the chief executive of the universe of Nebadon. [UB 33:4.5](/en/The_Urantia_Book/33#p4_5)

## II. THE MELCHIZEDEKS

1. The Father Melchizedek was created by the Creator Son and the Creative Spirit. [UB 35:1.1](/en/The_Urantia_Book/35#p1_1)
2. Melchizedeks are created by the Creator Son, Creative Spirit, and the original Father Melchizedek. [UB 35:1.3](/en/The_Urantia_Book/35#p1_3)
3. There are over ten million Melchizedeks in Nebadon. [UB 35:2.9](/en/The_Urantia_Book/35#p2_9)
4. They are called emergency Sons. Wherever special help is needed, there you will find them. [UB 35:4.2](/en/The_Urantia_Book/35#p4_2)
5. Melchizedeks patrol the entire local universe. [UB 35:2.3](/en/The_Urantia_Book/35#p2_3)

## III. THE VORONDADEKS

1. They are the Constellation Fathers—the Most Highs. [UB 35:5.1](/en/The_Urantia_Book/35#p5_1)
2. There are one million Vorondadeks in Nebadon. [UB 35:5.2](/en/The_Urantia_Book/35#p5_2)
3. One hundred of these Sons make up the supreme cabinet of the Creator Son. [UB 35:6.2](/en/The_Urantia_Book/35#p6_2)

## IV. THE LANONANDEKS

1. They perform many tasks, but are best known as System Sovereigns and Planetary Princes. [UB 35:8.1](/en/The_Urantia_Book/35#p8_1)
2. Nebadon started with twelve million Lanonandeks—in three groups:
   1. Primary. 709,841.
   2. Secondary. 10,234,601.
   3. Tertiary. 1,055,558. [UB 35:8.3](/en/The_Urantia_Book/35#p8_3)
3. Over seven hundred of these Sons have been lost in rebellion. [UB 35:9.6](/en/The_Urantia_Book/35#p9_6)

## V. THE LIFE CARRIERS

1. Life Carriers initiate life on the evolutionary worlds. [UB 36:3.1](/en/The_Urantia_Book/36#p1_2)
2. They catalyze the lifeless material. [UB 36:3.3](/en/The_Urantia_Book/36#p3_3)
3. The essentials of life come from the Universe Mother Spirit. [UB 36:3.4](/en/The_Urantia_Book/36#p3_4)
4. Each local system has a midsonite world on which a Melchizedek has functioned as a Life Carrier. [UB 36:4.1](/en/The_Urantia_Book/36#p4_1)

## VI. THE BRILLIANT EVENING STARS

1. There are two orders of Evening Stars—created and ascendant. They are special assistants to Gabriel. [UB 37:2.4](/en/The_Urantia_Book/37#p2_4)
2. There are 4,832 created and 8,809 ascendant Evening Stars. [UB 37:2.4](/en/The_Urantia_Book/37#p2_4)

## VII. ARCHANGELS

1. Archangels are largely dedicated to creature survival. [UB 37:3.2](/en/The_Urantia_Book/37#p3_2)
2. In recent times, a division of archangels was established on Urantia. [UB 37:3.3](/en/The_Urantia_Book/37#p3_3)

## VIII. THE MATERIAL SONS

1. These are the sex Sons and Daughters created by the Creator Son and stationed on the headquarters worlds of the local systems. [UB 51:1.1](/en/The_Urantia_Book/51#p1_1)
2. They are the permanent citizens of the local systems. [UB 51:1.6](/en/The_Urantia_Book/51#p1_6)

## IX. OTHER SONS

1. _Most High Assistants_—volunteers from outside the local universe. [UB 37:4.1](/en/The_Urantia_Book/37#p4_1)
2. _High Commissioners_—Spirit-fused mortals. [UB 37:5.1](/en/The_Urantia_Book/37#p5_1)
3. _Celestial Overseers_—a recruited teaching corps. [UB 37:6.1](/en/The_Urantia_Book/37#p6_1)
4. _Mansion World Teachers_—glorified cherubim. [UB 37:7.1](/en/The_Urantia_Book/37#p7_1)
5. _Spironga_—spirit helpers of the local universe. [UB 37:10.2](/en/The_Urantia_Book/37#p10_2)
