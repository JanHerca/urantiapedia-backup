Author: **William S. Sadler**

1. Total infinity exists as seven Absolutes:
	1. The Universal Father.
	2. The Eternal Son.
	3. The Infinite Spirit.
	4. The Isle of Paradise.
	5. The Deity Absolute.
	6. The Universal Absolute.
	7. The Unqualified Absolute. [UB 104:3.5](/en/The_Urantia_Book/104#p3_5)
2. _The Deity Absolute_ is the all-powerful activator. [UB 0:11.4](/en/The_Urantia_Book/0#p11_4)
3. _The Unqualified Absolute_ is nonpersonal, extradivine, and undeified. [UB 0:11.7](/en/The_Urantia_Book/0#p11_7) It dominates nether Paradise and upholds the physical universe.  11:2.8](/en/The_Urantia_Book/11#p1_2)
4. _The Universal Absolute_ equalizes tensions between: finite and infinite; potentials and actuals; Paradise and space; time and eternity; man and God.  [UB 0:11.13](/en/The_Urantia_Book/0#p11_13)
5. The First Source and Center is the Volitional Absolute; the Second Source and Center, the Personality Absolute. [UB 6:1.2](/en/The_Urantia_Book/6#p1_2)
6. The First Source and Center is the absolute reality which embraced all potentials and gave origin to all actuals.  [UB 115:3.6](/en/The_Urantia_Book/115#p3_6)
7. The Absolutes of potentiality operate upon the eternal level of the cosmos. On subabsolute levels the Supreme and the Ultimate function. [UB 115:3.13](/en/The_Urantia_Book/115#p3_13)
